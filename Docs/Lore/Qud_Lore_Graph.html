Caves of Qud

The world of Caves of Qud is a science-fantasy planet a billion years after a fallen interstellar civilisation. The Eaters of Earth imprisoned the god Ptoh on behalf of an interstellar federation called the Coven. The Coven later levied an injunction on Qud — and the Gyre began.
