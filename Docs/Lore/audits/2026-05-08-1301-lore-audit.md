## 3. Faction asymmetry — Pale Curation (rotation)

The Pale Curation is named in the bible as a
faction (L47 *"a Pale Curation vault"*; L95 *"a
Pale Curation facility"*) but the working-form
text has not been built. The audit-pile holds
*Curation* obliquely across many sittings (Depot
11; cataloguer Auch; first-bench Sevra; plate-
cards; raked-light photography; standing-shelf;
filing-decline). This sitting lands the working-
form for one filing-class and an etymology for one
of its canonised registers.

### Etymology — *prov.*

The Curation's filing-shorthand *prov.* derives
from the pre-cataclysm working-vocabulary's
**provisional**, the bench-clerk's mark for *not
yet sorted into final shelf-position*. The
Curation retained the abbreviation across the
cataclysm without retaining the bench-clerk's
hierarchy that *prov.* once anticipated *final*
in. The working-form's only living trace of
*final* is the standing-shelf, which is reached
by silent first-bench assent rather than by
working-form promotion. A Curation cataloguer
asked what *prov.* originally anticipated answers:
*the bench-clerk's final-shelf*. The cataloguer
does not know the final-shelf is no longer named.
The Curation holds the abbreviation across a
substrate-shift the working-form has not named.
*Prov.* is the slate's fourth lexical-substrate-
shift token after *stand* (Concord), *line*
(Brine), and *patina* (Glassblown). The triple
becomes a quadruple this sitting; the family
extends without extending the slate.

### The double-decline working-form

When a piece is brought to a Curation depot for
filing and *both* originating bodies refuse to
authorise the filing, the Curation's working-form
is the **double-decline** entry: a single working-
shelf card carrying the piece's intake-number,
both bodies' filing-declines (each in its own
working-register, not summarised), and the
cataloguer's signed *anti-edge intake; double-
decline filed prov.* sentence at card-foot. The
double-decline is the Curation's only working-
form for filing arbitrated pieces — pieces whose
provenance is held by the slate's anti-edge. The
working-form does not arbitrate between the two
declines; it holds them adjacent on the same
card, in the working-tradition's only register
for *both bodies are correct on their own
working-bench*.

### The standing-shelf-held register

A double-decline card whose iron-piece is read
across multiple working-decades by the depot's
first-bench is filed as **standing-shelf-held** —
the working-form for an anti-edge piece whose
arbitrated provenance has settled into the
cataloguer-tradition's working-knowledge without
either originating body's working-form admitting
it. The register's verb-form is *to hold the
standing-shelf* — the cataloguer-tradition's
working-discipline for keeping a double-declined
piece visible across working-decades without
arbitrating its provenance. *Standing-shelf-held*
is the cluster's fifteenth position (cf. §2.3
above); the verb *hold* now spans five working-
forms across all five bodies. The Curation's hold
is the only one whose object is *the act of not-
arbitrating*; the other four hold disciplines
(line, roster, stood, cooling). The Curation's
discipline is the discipline of not-disciplining.

## 4. Contradictions and bleeds

**Bleed 1 — bible L208 *"two cosmic-scale
entities that genuinely cannot understand"* vs.
the anti-edge's *mutual-arbitration*.** The bible's
authorial voice frames the Choir-Palimpsest
relation as *non-comprehension* (cosmic-tier);
the slate now distinguishes non-comprehension
from arbitration. The Brine-Thermoclaves
working-forms *do* comprehend each other —
arbitration requires comprehension; one cannot
name another's reading as wrong without first
reading it. The slate's anti-edge is *mutual-
comprehension-that-arbitrates*; the slate's seam-
edge is *parallel-non-arbitration*; the bible's
Choir-Palimpsest relation is *non-comprehension-
without-arbitration* (a third position). Suggested
L208 supplement: *"(Three relation-forms on the
slate: seam-edges (mutual-comprehension, non-
arbitration), anti-edges (mutual-comprehension,
mutual-arbitration), and the Choir-Palimpsest
non-comprehension (no comprehension, no
arbitration). The first two are mid-tier; the
third is cosmic-tier and is the bible's central
axis. The cosmic-tier holds the slate's only non-
comprehension relation.)"*

**Bleed 2 — bible L24 *some try to ignore* vs.
the Curation's *prov.*-etymology.** The bible
enumerates four cataclysm-relations:
preservation, consumption, ignorance, production.
The Curation's *prov.* is a *fifth*: the working-

the Concord's *stand* and the Brine's *line*. The
Curation reads its own *prov.* as a routine
filing-mark; it does not read the abbreviation as
a substrate-shift artifact. Suggested L24
supplement (extending 1201's): *"(The lexical-

itself — the bench-clerk's hierarchy that prov.

working-form reaches no final.)"*
