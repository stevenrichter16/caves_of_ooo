## 2. Depth audit

### 2.1 Mythic-historical stratification

**Quote (1201 §2.4):** *"A piece that has been
declared canon-cooled and subsequently cracks,
deforms, fogs, or otherwise changes is a re-warming."*

**Strong.** Re-warming canon: the medium refuses the
watch's reading after closure.

**Thin.** Re-warming is a **medium-event**. The held-
back's unsanctioned watch is a **cadence-event**. The
bible has not named the distinction.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Glassblown Remnant,
append after the re-warming paragraph):**

> *The body's discipline locates its working-claims
> at two separable junctions:* the medium *(the glass
> itself, declared cooled or re-warmed by the watch's
> reading) and* the cadence *(the working-time of the
> watch, three witnesses speaking in turn at one
> attended moment). A re-warming is a medium-failure
> after a successful cadence. A held-back's
> unsanctioned watching, alone and continuous in the
> cool-quarter on uncoolable pieces, is a cadence
> conducted outside discipline upon a medium
> discipline has abandoned. The first the body
> accommodates by exception-form (re-warming); the
> second the body holds invisible. A senior asked
> whether the held-back's watching is a watch
> answers:* "the body does not call it a watch. the
> body does not call it nothing. the body does not
> call it." *The triple-negation is the body's
> working-form for an irregularity it locates at
> neither junction by name and at both by working-
> tradition.*

### 2.2 Unreliable in-world authorship

**Quote (1201 §2.2):** *"A marked record by an
unpoured hand is, in working reading, a forgery of
authorship."*

**Strong.** Unpoured-authorship prohibition canon.

**Thin.** The held-back is not unpoured in the
working-sense — they are *held back* from first-
firing, a class the discipline has not classed for
authorship at all. The discipline's silence permits
a working-form the discipline has not noticed.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Glassblown Remnant,
new sidebar "The watcher's residue"):**

> *A piece kept on a cool-quarter shelf for ten or
> more working-years, observed daily by a single
> held-back, accumulates a faint visual patina at the
> rim or shoulder where the held-back's gaze rests
> longest — in field-cataloguer practice, an off-axis
> whorl no wider than a thumbnail, visible only in
> raked light. Partly oil-trace from second-light
> handling, partly an optical residue the Curation

> of a* characteristic whorl-shape *across pieces
> watched by the same held-back, photographable at
> sufficient resolution to identify the watcher by
> an inscription the watcher's hand has not made.
> The body has no working-form for the patina. By
> the discipline, it is not authored. By the
> Curation's filing, it is the only physical record
> of the held-back's continuous attention.*

Authorship triangulates *across* the body's membrane:
pieces (batch-glyph + pourer's-mark, working); beads
(apprentice unmarked-count, surrendered); patina
(held-back's unauthored residue, **carried by the
medium itself across the body's discipline**). The
medium remembers the cadence the discipline does not
file.

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Glassblown Remnant (the rear shelf, the patina-line)

> ### The patina-line
>
> The cool-quarter's *rear shelf* (1201 §3.1) holds
> uncoolables. Working-tradition, not codified in any
> standing-shelf, divides the rear at a single
> unmarked line: the **patina-line**. To one side,
> uncoolables watched daily by a held-back for fewer
> than ten working-years (the *near rear*); to the
> other, uncoolables watched daily by a single held-
> back for ten or more working-years (the *far
> rear*). The held-back, in the corner, knows where
> the line falls. The rotating apprentice is taught
> only that the far rear is walked at the same pace
> as the near rear — the working-form for *do not
> slow down*.
>
> ### Settled
>
> A piece that has acquired a recognisable patina is,
> by working-tradition, *settled* — a working-form
> the discipline does not recognise. The patina is
> never spoken of in the public hall. A senior who
> notices a settled piece may, in pressing-clean
> register on her own retirement morning, name it to
> the apprentice taking her station — once, by shelf-
> position only. The naming is the only working-form
> for *I have seen it; you may see it*.
>
> ### The cataloguer's walk
>
> A Curation cataloguer admitted to the cool-quarter
> is permitted to walk the near rear once and the far
> rear not at all. A cataloguer filing a far-rear
> observation has *misread the line* — filed as
> *position-error*, never *trespass*. The held-back,
> on cataloguer visits, has been observed to incline
> their head toward the patina-line by a working-
> degree the cataloguer's pace tells the cataloguer
> to read. The reading is the held-back's only
> canonical instruction to outsiders.

**Bleed 3 — gesture-watch vs. 1601 Concord
seam.** The held-back's left-hand-palm-down at a
suspected re-warming (1201 §5) is a watch-conclusion
gesture conducted alone. The Concord senior's seam is
a third-witness function conducted *as the body* for

working-organs the discipline cannot subject. The
Concord's seam is destroyed at pressing-clean (1601
§2.5); the held-back's gesture is **inscribed onto
the piece** in the patina, where the body cannot
reach to delete it. The Glassblown's analogue to
pressing-clean fails: the medium keeps the cadence
the body will not file. This is the cleanest two-
axis statement in the cycle.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted (§2.1):** *"A re-warming is a medium-failure
after a successful cadence. A held-back's
unsanctioned watching, alone and continuous in the
cool-quarter on uncoolable pieces, is a cadence
conducted outside discipline upon a medium
discipline has abandoned."*

Working-modern register. *Medium-failure* and
*cadence conducted* are coined plain compounds,
lower-case, no archaism. *After* and *upon* are
contemporary; no fronted nominal, no *whereby /
betwixt / doth*. The two-axis distinction is phrased
as a structural claim, not a maxim.

**TES rewrite (forbidden):** *"That Re-Warming which
followeth a Cooling honestly Watched is the failing
of the Glass after the failing of the Time hath been
made well; that Watch which the Held-Back doth
conduct, alone and without ceasing, in the Cold
Quarter, is the failing of the Time without the
failing of the Glass."* Tells: capitalised *Re-
Warming / Cooling / Watched / Held-Back / Cold
Quarter / Glass / Time*; *followeth*, *doth*, *hath*;
fronted *That Re-Warming which*; archaic predicate
*made well*. Actual sentence is none of these.

**Pass.**
