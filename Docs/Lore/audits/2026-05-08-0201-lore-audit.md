## 2. Depth audit

### 2.1 Mythic-historical stratification

**Quote (2101 §2.1):** *"the seam-bench is the
hall's working-furniture; its wear is generated
inward-by-working over Concord working-generations,
and the bench is replaced only at the working-life-
end of the hall's working-floor."*

**Strong.** Inward-generate canon as bench-wear-
across-generations.

**Thin.** The bench's *retirement* has been treated
as a hypothetical — the Sev. year 314 hall 7 re-
floor was raised as candidate but never described
as a Concord working-day. What actually happens
when a bench retires is unwritten. The Communion's
parallel (chart-overboard at first-voyage morning,
1501 §2.2) is canon at the working-form level: the
chart goes overboard with a stone. The Concord's
parallel — what happens to the retired seam-bench
— must be canon at the same working-form depth or
the directions are not symmetrically defined.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Saccharine
Concord, append after 2101 §2.1):**

> *The retired seam-bench is splintered in-hall by
> a junior of the splintering-roster. The
> splintering takes a single working-day at the
> hall's working-ledge; the wood is then stacked
> for the winter-furnace's wood-rank. The wear-line
> is destroyed at the saw-stroke; the wood is
> destroyed weeks later at the furnace. Two working-
> forms, two working-clauses, both in-hall. The
> bench-section never leaves.*

The bench's working-life-end is **two-stage in-hall
extinction**. Inward-generate's exit is *splinter
then burn*, both inside the hall's working-walls.

### 2.2 Unreliable in-world authorship

**Quote (2101 §2.2):** *"the drawer-bundle is the
silence-archive; the hall's working-form authors no
working-record outside the bundle, and the bundle
authors nothing — it accumulates by working-day's
deferral."*

**Strong.** Drawer-bundle as accumulation-not-
authorship canon.

**Thin.** The Concord's authorship-doctrine has
been thinly defined relative to the Communion's
five-mode refinement (0101 §2.2: cadence / count /
trust-as-wear / sea-time / glance). The Concord's
working-day's-deferral is one mode; the seam-
bench's wear-line is another (working-by-bench);
the splintering's saw-stroke is a third (extinction-
authoring); the splintering junior's saw-hand
callus is a fourth (extinction-register-by-flesh).
Four modes for the Concord, none of them outside
the hall's working-walls.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Saccharine
Concord, append after the drawer-bundle paragraph):**

> *The Concord authors by four working-forms, all
> body-internal:* working-day's-deferral *(drawer-
> bundle accumulation);* working-by-bench *(seam-
> bench wear-accumulation);* extinction-by-saw-
> stroke *(splintering's wear-line destruction);*
> register-by-flesh *(splintering junior's saw-hand
> callus). No Concord working-form authors outside
> the hall. The hall's working-walls are the
> authorship-boundary; the boundary admits no
> exception.*

The Concord is the only body whose **working-walls
are the boundary** in the literal masonry sense.
Communion authorship has no walls (sea); Curation,
none; Glassblown, kiln-bound but the medium walks;
Thermoclave, compound-bound but the generation
walks. Only at the Concord do hall **and** working-
form together hold the boundary.

### 2.3 Mutating motifs across cultures

**Hold-and-name.** 0101's *outside-body form-absent*
motif meets its **structural opposite**: the
Concord's *in-hall extinction*. The same axis
question — *what does a body do with its working-
medium at the medium's working-life-end?* — admits
two shapes. The Communion's working-life-end is
**two-form** (overboard with stone, OR keepsake to
outside hand); the Concord's is **two-stage**
(splinter then burn). Two-form is a *fork* — medium
goes one of two ways at the same working-day —
admitting the outside-body branch. Two-stage is a
*sequence* — the same medium goes through two
destructions in order — and both stages are in-
hall. Glassblown crucible, Curation working-shelf,
Thermoclave compound-tackle: working-life-end-form
not yet canon at 0201.

### 2.4 Cosmology with internal mechanical logic

**Quote (0101 §2.4):** *"five cadence-authorities
hold the body's witness-function: the rendezvous
song-lap; the bead-tackle count; the chart-carry-
forward; the sea-time threshold; the keepsake's

**Strong.** Five Communion cadence-authorities;

**Thin.** The Concord's cadence-authorities have

silence threshold** (working-day's-deferral
authority); the **seam-tool count** (no-bench,
count-by-handing); the **bench-wear-carry-forward**

working-life-end, **what corresponds to the
keepsake's outside-life?** The Concord has *no
outside-body cadence-authority*. The fifth slot is
**occupied by absence** — the splintering's saw-
stroke fills the slot from the inside.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Saccharine
Concord, append after the four-modes paragraph):**

> *Five cadence-authorities hold the hall's witness-
> function:* hall-silence threshold; seam-tool count;
> bench-wear-carry-forward; working-day-end
> threshold; *and* **bench-extinction's saw-stroke**

The Concord pins **forbids-extension**; the
Communion pins **admits-extension**. Three bodies
unsorted along this axis at 0201.

hall-silence threshold; seam-tool count; bench-

authorities, all hall-internal; the fifth is the
only one tied to a single working-day across
working-generations.

### What the body does not do

The body does not extend the seam-bench's wear-line
outward. The bench is not gifted, kept, fragmented
for token, buried, given at retirement, or
transferred between halls. The hall 7 re-floor of
Sev. year 314 produced one retired bench-section,
splintered by splintering-junior Beck-arr in autumn
of that year, burned across working-winter 314–315.
The candidate raised in 0101 (junior keeping a
discarded bench-section) is a false premise the

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted (§3, the splintering working-day):** *"the
splintering-junior carries her saw, the hall's
working-ledge is set, the bench is presented in
seam-junior working-fashion (single hand-touch on
the bench's working-edge, no salutation), and the
splintering begins at the working-day's first

Working-modern register. *Splintering-junior*,
*saw*, *working-ledge*, *seam-junior working-
fashion*, *single hand-touch*, *no salutation*,
*working-day's first working-hour* — plain craft-
clerical compounds, lower-case, no archaism.
*Carries*, *is set*, *is presented*, *begins* are
contemporary; no fronted nominal, no *hath / lieth /
unto / verily*.

**TES rewrite (forbidden):** *"The Splintering-
Junior beareth her Saw; the Working-Ledge of the
Hall is set; the Bench is presented unto her in
the Seam-Junior's Working-Fashion (a single Hand-
touch upon the Bench's Working-Edge, with no
Salutation); and at the Working-Day's First
Working-Hour the Splintering doth begin."* Tells:
capitalised *Splintering-Junior / Saw / Working-
Ledge / Bench / Hand-touch / Working-Edge /
Salutation / Working-Hour*; *beareth, presented
unto, doth begin*; fronted *the Working-Ledge of
the Hall*; *upon* in lieu of *on*. Actual sentence
