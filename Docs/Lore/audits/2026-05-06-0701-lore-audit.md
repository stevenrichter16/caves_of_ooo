## 3. Faction asymmetry — Thermoclaves rotate in (consolidation)

The Thermoclaves have been rotated once (0301) and referenced
five times (0301, 0404, 0407, 0501, 0607). Sitting 21
**consolidates** rather than invents. **No audit-invented NPCs.**

### Etymology

*Thermoclave* in chain-speech (the chain has not produced an
etymology) is read here as a working-grammar compound: *thermo-*
(working-heat, the heat of a pour) plus *-clave* (a *closed
chamber*; a vessel sealed against external air for the duration
of a working-event). A Thermoclave is *a sealed working-chamber
of working-heat* — both the artifact (the forge enclosure
chamber) and the people who tend it. A faction named for a
working-tool, the way the Concord names itself for a working-
relation. The bible has not used this compound-reading; the
chain ought to.

### Consolidated working-rules (already implicit)

  - **Forge-pair kin-prohibition (0301).** A Thermoclave's
    forge-pair partner cannot be kin. Two persons share a forge
    for a working-life; the bond is named, ritual, and
    structurally not-family. The reasoning parallels the
    Concord's kin-recusal (0607): *family cannot hold the
    procedural register over family.*

  - **Dating-grammar (0501-derived).** Two date-words: *pre-loss*
    and *standing-loss*. Year-numbers are appended to
    *standing-loss* (e.g. *standing-loss 412* — 412 years after
    the cataclysm). *Pre-loss* takes no year-number except by
    counterparty's tradition; the Thermoclaves do not date
    pre-loss internally.

  - **Bone-ash strip witnessing (0607-derived).** A working
    forge maintains a strip of bone-ash pinned at *the second
    stone* (a named architectural feature of the Thermoclave
    forge). The strip's colour-gradient reads the working-quality
    of the day's pours; a forge-pair reads its own day off the
    strip at close-of-work.

  - **Welcoming (this sitting, §2.7-derived).** Objects entering
    the forge are spoken to before the first heat. Pre-loss
    objects by name, standing-loss objects by date. Witnessed
    by the forge-pair; recorded in the workshop's working-log.

### Mourning practice — the unshared pour

When one of a forge-pair dies, the surviving partner performs *the
unshared pour* — a single working-pour at the dead partner's
position at the second-stone, witnessed by no one but the
surviving partner. The pour is not productive (the metal is
returned to scrap); the working-time is. After the unshared pour,
the surviving partner leaves the forge for one working-year before
being assigned a new forge-pair. The Thermoclaves believe a forge
should not produce in the year a forge-pair has been broken; the
working-year is the metal cooling.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §5 forge-record):**

> *"A working object remembers what it worked. The bell remembers
> ringing. The bell-bronze remembers being struck."*

Working register, working-grammar. *Remembers* is the flat working
verb a forge-pair would use about a metal under heat — the heat
makes the metal's prior work legible (grain, crystal-history,
strain-pattern), and a forge-master describes that legibility as
*remembering* without flagging it as figurative. *Bell* and
*bell-bronze* and *struck* are working-trade terms. Lower-case
throughout. The triple-clause structure — *what it worked /
ringing / struck* — is the kind a senior forge-master would
actually drill into an apprentice: same shape, three scales
(general principle, the bell as object, the bronze as substrate).

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Verily, the Bronze of the Bell hath a
Memory of its own — a long Echo of the Ringing it once knew, and
a deeper Sorrow of the Striker's Hand, that ancient Pain which the
Heat alone may yet reveal unto the patient Smith."* Tells:
capitalisations on *Bronze*, *Bell*, *Memory*, *Echo*, *Ringing*,
*Sorrow*, *Hand*, *Pain*, *Heat*, *Smith*; *verily* and *hath* and
*unto* (KJV-archaic register); *that ancient Pain* (appositional
mythologisation); *deeper Sorrow* (figurative anatomy of an
abstract noun). The actual sentence: *working*, *object*, *bell*,
*bell-bronze*, *struck*, *ringing*, *remembers* — seven lower-case
working terms, the principle (memory-as-legibility-of-prior-work)
doing the rhetorical work.

Pass.
