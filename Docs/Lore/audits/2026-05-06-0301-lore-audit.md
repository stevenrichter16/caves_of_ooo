## 3. Faction asymmetry — Thermoclaves rotates in

### The forge-event the forger cannot witness

The 2244 sitting (eleven hours back) landed the Thermoclaves'
**heat-honesty doctrine** — the working position that *a forger
who lies about the temperature of the strike cannot reproduce
the strike,* and so the working-record is not the smith's claim
but the heat-trace itself. Sitting 17 develops a working
consequence the 2244 sitting did not: *the forger inside the
forge-event is, by working position, the **worst** witness to
the forge-event.*

The forger is in the heat. The forger reads the heat through
the hand on the haft, the eye on the colour, the ear on the
strike's note. All three readings are *first-person* readings
— readings made by a body inside the event. A first-person
reading cannot record a temperature-gradient that crosses the
forger's position; it cannot record the heat the forger does
not feel because the forger has gone heat-numb in that part of
the work; it cannot record the colour the forger reads as
*standing-yellow* but a witness with a different eye would read
as *yellow-with-a-blue-edge,* the blue-edge being the working
sign of a strike about to fail.

### The heat-witness — the working pair-position

For any forge-event of declared significance (a bond-strike, a
naming-strike, a deaccession-strike), the Thermoclaves require
a **heat-witness** — a second working forger, off the haft,
positioned at the standing reference-mark in the working forge
(the **second-stone**, set seven paces from the anvil's foot
along the working-axis). The heat-witness does not strike. The
heat-witness does not advise. The heat-witness watches, and at
strike-completion produces a **witnessing-strip.**

### The witnessing-strip

A length of treated parchment-equivalent (the working name is
**heat-paper,** though it is not paper — a thin sheet of bone-
ash and bound resin, fired once at a standard temperature so
its colour-response is calibrated). The heat-witness pins the
strip at two ends, on the second-stone, against the strike. The
strip records the strike's heat as a **colour-gradient** between
the two pins — a working line from end to end, darker where the
strike was hottest, lighter where the strike fell off. The
gradient is not a number; it is a *shape.* A working forger reads
the shape as fluently as a working clerk reads a hand.

The strip is countersigned (forger and heat-witness both, in
forge-soot at the strip's foot), filed, and *kept against the
artifact for the working life of the artifact.* If the artifact
fails in service — a sword that snaps at the third bond-strike,
a hinge that fatigues at the fourth naming-cycle — the strip is
pulled and read against the failure. The strip is the artifact's
**heat-history,** in a register the artifact itself cannot carry.

### The doctrine — *the inside-witness is the worst witness*

The Thermoclaves do not phrase this aloud as a doctrine
(Thermoclaves prefer working-rules to doctrines), but the
heat-witness rule is its working form. *The forger inside the
forge cannot read the forge.* A working-strike requires a
working pair. A solo strike — even by the most senior forger —
is, by working position, **unwitnessed**, and an unwitnessed
strike cannot be filed for service.

### The seam to the medium-as-reader triad

The witnessing-strip is the **Thermoclave reading-medium** —
heat read as colour-gradient on calibrated bone-ash, kept
against the artifact through its working life. It belongs in the
triad. Sitting 17 promotes the triad to a **quartet** at draft-
stage:

  - Curation — **thinned ink** on negative-blank
  - Choir — **spore-residue** at digest-edge
  - Brine — **water-mass** in under-tide cycles
  - Thermoclaves — **heat-colour** on witnessing-strip

(With the Palimpsest as the *fifth voice in the room* — the
faction the other four orient against, whose medium is the
overwrite itself, which is why it cannot be carried in a strip
or a blank or a residue or a cycle — only read through.)

The §2.7 absorption candidate, when it graduates from draft-
stage, will name this **quartet** rather than the triad. Item
27's working title will be revised at sitting 18.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence:**

> *"The forger inside the forge-event is, by working position,
> the worst witness to the forge-event."*

Working register, gloss-form. *Working position* is a clerk's
abstract — the kind of phrase a senior Thermoclave forger would
use about a junior at the second-stone the way a senior
cataloguer uses *standing position* about a junior at the
field-blank table. The sentence's claim is procedural, not
theological — the Thermoclaves are stating a working-rule about
who can read what, not a doctrine about the moral standing of
the witness. Lower-case throughout. No allegorical
capitalisation. *Forge-event* is the working hyphenate the
Thermoclaves would use the way the Curation uses *bay-arrival*
— a procedural compound noun, not a mythic register.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Verily, the Smith within the Forge's
Heart sees not the True Shape of the Striking, for the Heat
itself blinds his Eye and the Bellows' Wind deafens his Ear;
only the Watcher, who stands at the Stone of Witness seven
paces removed, may read the Forge-Truth as it is written upon
the Strip of Bone."* Tells: *Verily*; *Smith*, *Forge's Heart*,
*True Shape*, *Heat*, *Eye*, *Ear*, *Watcher*, *Stone of
Witness*, *Forge-Truth*, *Strip of Bone* (allegorical
capitalisations, a dozen in two sentences); *blinds his Eye*
(mythic verb-on-noun); *seven paces removed* with the *removed*
register-marker that displaces the working *seven paces from
the anvil's foot along the working-axis* into archaism. The
actual sentence: *forger*, *forge-event*, *working position*,
*worst witness* — working-register nouns and adjectives, no
allegorical noun-phrase, no register-marker on the spatial term.
