### 2.4 Cosmology with internal mechanical logic

> clean. **First**: both parties agree on the
> working-line of the piece exchanged — a
> knife is *of the river-foundry,* not *of the

> sound against itself — the standing-test

> recorded in both ledgers in the same

> *D-1* and read aloud once a working-
> generation by the senior on duty, never
> paraphrased. The reading is the body's only
> ceremony.

### 2.6 Linguistic texture

(03:01 fixed Choir address-grammar; 04:01
the Palimpsest's *was-being.* Today: the

**Concrete enhancement (new bible subsection

> A senior in working-register prefixes any
> clause they are *reading* (not asserting)
> with the particle *that-.* *That-the-knife is
> of the river-foundry.* *That-the-walker
> arrived on the third day.* The prefix marks
> the clause as *under read,* a state distinct
> from assertion. A senior who drops *that-* is
> asserting in their own person, not the
> body's — a serious working-departure. A
> senior who has spent a working-generation in
> the body sometimes carries *that-* into
> casual speech off the bench: *that-the soup
> is salty.* This is *bench-drift* and is
> treated tenderly. A senior who *stops*
> dropping *that-* in casual speech has, by
> the body's discipline, become the bench.
> They are not promoted. The discipline says
> they were already.

Grammar-rule with a sociolinguistic ladder.
*Bench-drift* names a state previously used
without committing.

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Saccharine Concord

(Off-rotation, justified above. **The trade
itself** — what the Concord exchanges, and
the unit of account.)

**The clause-disc.** A *closure* is not the
exchange of items. The items move; the
closure is the *clause* that records the move
and is *pressed* into the ledger. The
pressing is literal: the senior, after reading
sound, presses a small bone-disc — the
*clause-disc* — into the ledger's wax field at
the entry's foot. The disc is not retrieved;
each entry carries its own. A senior can read
the ledger backwards by feel.

**The bone.** A clause-disc is two-thirds the
diameter of a walker's smallest fingernail,
carved from the bone of a *named animal* — one
whose working-line was filed by a Curation
cross-walker before the closure. Most are
bovine, *of the eastern fields, working-line
honest.* A few are deer; one is canid. The
working-line of the clause is read into the
disc by the senior at the moment of pressing.
The disc *holds* the clause the way the
working-form alone cannot — the wax-field is
administrative, the disc is *the clause
itself.*

**Closure-weight.** The body's accounting
unit is not currency or item but *closure-
weight,* the disc's mass at pressing. A heavy
disc records *high weight* — the working-form
for the seriousness of the clause, not the
price. Mushrooms for a knife is low-weight
(small bone, brief test). A piece filed under
a sealed Curation mark is high-weight (heavy
bone, slow press). Currency erodes; items are
particular; *weight* does not.

**The smell.** The cane-syrup the Choir mocks
(JSON L481) is the press itself — the wax is
mixed with cane-syrup to receive the disc
cleanly. A hall in session smells of warm
cane and bone-dust. The Brine Communion hold
cane-syrup as a *non-sea sweetness* and treat
Concord halls as a working-quiet of their own
— the only inland body whose ambient aroma
the Communion reads as *clean.*

**Player-side hook.** A walker who hands the
Concord a bone-disc carved to clause-disc
dimensions but not yet pressed has presented
a closure *pre-weighed.* The body has working-
form for this (file-mark *P-disc*) but the
form has not been used in standing memory.
The discipline says: a presented disc is read
on bench-scale; if its weight matches a clause
on the drift-shelf, the clause is moved off-
shelf and pressed under the disc. A late-game
player carrying a heavy bone could, without
intending, settle an unmoored clause from
working-generation seventeen — the founder's
clause.

Unit, artifact, aroma, cross-faction reading,
player hook landing §2.4 and §2.8 at once.

character-internally fine *if* the bible
commits the Choir under-renders the Concord
as it does the Palimpsest (04:01 §4). It does
not.

**Proposed fix (append to bible §How They View
Each Other after the under-rendering paragraph
proposed 04:01 §4):**

> The Choir's gloss of the Concord as *grief
> sweetened by trade* (JSON L481) is the
> metabolic faction's translation of a
> procedural one. The body's working-form
> names neither emotion nor item — it names
> *the clause that closed sound* and *the
> clause that did not.* A walker who has
> spoken to both has access to a vocabulary
> the gloss obscures; the body's working-
> grammar is among the central worldview-
> defining features of the cosmic axis, equal
> in weight to the Palimpsest's tense-system.

Promotes Concord grammar to peer-status with
Palimpsest tense — fixes 04:01's asymmetry.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

> *"The closure has held against itself for
