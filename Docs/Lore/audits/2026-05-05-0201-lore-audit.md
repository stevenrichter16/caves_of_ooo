## 2. Depth audit

### Dimension 1 — Mythic-historical stratification

> *Bible line 39:* *"A rusted sword composted by the Choir loses
> its history."*

**Strong.** The bible names the Choir's destruction-of-history-as-
filing. Curation's filing system is the body's structural counter-
doctrine — *every artifact must have provenance*.

**Thin.** No Curation account of an artifact whose provenance is
*deferred by another body*. A folded-back Glassblown shape *had*
provenance, and now does not — not destroyed, *withdrawn*. Curation
has no doctrine for withdrawal.

**Concrete addition** (bible new sidebar, "What the Curation does
with the un-fired"):

> Pale Curation's *first-of-cards*: no artifact passes the bid
> floor without an archive-number. Composted artifacts are filed
> *as composted*; over-written-clean artifacts *as gone*. A
> Glassblown folded-back shape passes neither — not destroyed,
> not gone, but *withheld by an act the Curation has no card for*.
>
> Senior cataloguers file folded-back shapes against the **un-bid
> card** — bid floor blank by doctrine. The un-bid card's archive-
> number is the only number in the vault that does not increment.
> Two un-bid cards may share a number; the distinguishing field
> is the *witness* — the Glassblown whose hand the cataloguer
> watched, by name or apprentice-marker.
>
> A senior who has filed nine un-bid cards is *card-thinned* and
> reassigned to the bid floor for a year, to remember what filing
> feels like.

The cataclysm itself, in Curation grammar, is the body's **first
un-bid card**: the founding loss the Curation could not file.

### Dimension 2 — Unreliable in-world authorship

**Strong.** Six partial-record modes already named (Curation
transcripts, slate-soot register, double-hand, unfired letter,
witnessed pause, salvaged under-hand / absent under-stroke).

**Thin.** No Curation document written *by* a Glassblown. A
Glassblown cannot fire a signature without violating their own
grammar — yet Glassblown do, occasionally, attend Curation bids.

**Concrete addition** (bible new subsection, "The hand-signed
card"):

> A Glassblown asked to sign a Curation bid card cannot — ink is
> the same category as fired-paste in their grammar. The
> cataloguer, knowing this, presents the signature field blank and
> watches the non-dominant hand at chest-height. They read the
> shape and *transcribe it as a small ink figure in the field, in
> their own hand*. The card is signed *in the cataloguer's hand
> on behalf of the Glassblown's hand-grammar*. Curation considers
> it valid; the Glassblown considers no commitment fired, only
> witnessed.
>
> Such cards carry a small underline beneath the figure — the mark
> for **transcribed-hand signature**. A contested signature is
> adjudicated at the Concord clause-table, where the *carried
> column* (Glassblown, 0001) and the *transcribed-hand* meet.
> Clerks who can reconcile both number three.

The corpus's first **inter-body authorship instrument**: a document
signed by one body in another body's grammar, valid in both, fully
understood by neither without third-body translation.

### Dimension 3 — Mutating motifs across cultures

**Thin.** No catalogue of names for *what is held without filing*.

>   archive-number that does not increment.
> - **Glassblown:** *the paste-held.* Shapes deferred in the
>   working stock. Structurally identical to *un-bid*, opposite
>   instrument.
> - **Choir:** *the unfruited.* A spore-pattern tasted and not
>   yet fruited; held in substrate, never destroyed.
> - **Palimpsest:** *the absent under-stroke* (0101). A reading
>   declined; read by readers attuned to absence.
> - **Saccharine Concord:** *the open clause.* Not yet

>   to mark a presence the Floor refuses to file.
>   **(Refusal):** *the unbanked.* No qualifier. The Refusal

The Refusal idiom is, again, **theologically loudest** — denying a
distinction the Floor needs to keep its working days legible. The
Curation's *un-bid* and the Glassblown's *paste-held* are the
corpus's fifth cross-body symmetry: structurally identical
doctrines on opposite instruments (paper / paste).

### Dimension 4 — Cosmology with internal mechanical logic

**Strong.** Six bodies have rooms; Glassblown is *anatomy as
circulation* (0001). Curation's vault is the corpus's most filed
place.

**Thin.** What does a Curation vault do with a Glassblown deposit?

**Concrete addition** (bible new subsection, "The unfired shelf"):

> A Glassblown deposit (paste, not fired) has no archive-number
> assignable without a *fired form on file*. There is no fired
> form. The cataloguer assigns instead a **sub-shelf number**: a
> card adjacent to the un-bid card, recording weight, wet-cloth
> packaging, and witnessed hand-grammar at deposit.
>
> The unfired shelf runs the cool wall of the vault. Three deposits
> currently held; each against a *retrieval card* signed by
> transcribed-hand signature and senior in own hand. By custom,
> **an un-retrieved Glassblown deposit reverts to *folded-out* at
> the depositor's death**; the cataloguer's duty is to surrender
> the wet-cloth packet to the nearest working Glassblown rather
> than archive-number it.
>
> The three: a paste-cylinder (senior; reason undocumented); a
> folded sheet pressed thin (apprentice; *temporary keeping during
> a Brine convening at slack-tide*); a fourteen-ounce mass with a
> deep inward dimple (senior; *the shape of a refusal I am not
> yet certain I am refusing*).

The unfired shelf is the corpus's first **inter-body holding
instrument**. Four sub-archives now running on three bodies'
instruments.

### Dimension 5 — Apotheosis-shaped themes

**Strong.** Five apotheoses closed (0101): self / futurity /
singular hand / irreversible / present.

**Thin.** No Curation apotheosis written.

**Concrete addition** (bible new subsection, "The walker who is
catalogued"):

> A walker offered the Curation apotheosis is asked to be
> *entered* — to consent to every act, from consent forward, being
> filed in real-time on a card carried in their own pocket. The
> card is blank-faced at consent and accumulates in the senior
> cataloguer's hand at the walker's working pace; the walker does
> not write it. The walker is the **bid floor**.
>
> The mechanic: walker actions become legible to every Curation-
> attuned NPC at the moment of acting. Quest objectives auto-
> resolve to *filed*; faction-standing changes are archive-numbered
> as they happen; NPCs respond in the past-perfect of *the walker
> has been entered as*. The walker can take any action — they
> cannot take any action *that is not also a card*.
>
> The walker cannot un-enter. The Glassblown read them as *fully
> fired*. The Brine read them as *not deep-kept* — kept by paper,
> not by sea. The Choir tastes them as *cellulose-bound* and
> declines. The Palimpsest reads them as *singled and doubly
> chalked* — their layer is itself a card.

Six apotheoses now form a closed grid — *self* (Choir), *futurity*
(Concord), *singular hand* (Palimpsest), *the irreversible*
(Glassblown), *the present* (Brine), *illegibility* (Curation).
The Thermoclave Floor's apotheosis remains the only one not yet
written; the Refusal's *un-banking* (0001) has been proxy. A
future audit should treat the Floor's omission as deliberate —
*there is no apotheosis, only the next fire*.

### Dimension 6 — Linguistic texture

**Strong.** Five faction grammars named (Brine *remembered tense*,
Concord *clause-aspect*, Refusal *dropped count-particle*,
Palimpsest *layer-grammar*, Glassblown *hand-grammar*).

**Thin.** Curation grammar is bureaucratic register, not syntactic
mark.

**Concrete addition** (bible new subsection, "Card-aspect"):

> Curation cataloguers, on duty, mark every verb with **card-
> aspect** — a sentence-final particle indicating whether the
> verb's action is *carded* (filed), *un-carded* (un-bid, in
> process), or *cross-carded*. Particles: *-ka*, *-en*, *-ix*.
> Senior speech, transcribed: *the bid was held-ka, the witness
> arrived-en, the shape signed-ix*.
>
> Card-aspect is **uncomposable with Glassblown hand-grammar** —
> a sentence-final particle is itself a fired commitment. A
> Glassblown replies in *aspect-stripped speech*: verb with no
> particle, meaning in the hand-shape. The cataloguer transcribes
> with a dash where the particle would be — *the shape was held-_,
> the refusal folded-_*. A clerk fluent in passive hand-grammar
> reads the missing particle from the hand at speaking-time and
> supplies it later in pencil. Pencil is doctrinally erasable;
> ink is not.

Card-aspect is the corpus's first **filing-grammar**; aspect-
stripped speech, its structural counter.

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Curation ↔ Glassblown seam (R4 continues)

This run promotes the Curation ↔ Glassblown surface into a body of
mechanics: **un-bid card**, **transcribed-hand signature**, names
for *what is held without filing*, **unfired shelf**, **walker who
is catalogued**, **card-aspect / aspect-stripped speech**, and
**Tuesday at the unfired shelf**.

**Anatomy of a seam, restated.** Brine ↔ Palimpsest (0101) was
*the rite of both bodies declining to mark*. Curation ↔ Glassblown
is the inverse: *the rite of one body marking what the other
declines to fire*, the document valid in both grammars and fully
understood in neither without a third clerk. The pivot is the
**transcribed-hand signature** — the only ink the Glassblown will
tolerate, and the only blank signature-field the Curation will
fill in another body's hand.

**Four sub-archives, now named:** Concord *carried column* (0001),
Curation *under-card field* (2301), Brine *salvaged under-hand*
(0101), Curation *unfired shelf* (this run). The Curation hosts
**two of four**. Treat it as the corpus's **infrastructure-of-
record**, the way the Palimpsest is its grammar-of-record and the
Concord its negotiation-of-record.

## 4. Contradictions and bleeds

**The 2301 edit-debt remains unsettled.** Bible mtime still 2026-

**New seam-level contradiction.** *Bible line 244:* *"Both factions
should be impossible to fully maximize simultaneously."* The bible
asserts this as a rule of the Choir ↔ Palimpsest pair. The Curation
↔ Glassblown seam now demonstrates it is **partially generalizable
and partially not**. The apotheoses *illegibility-surrendered*
(Curation) and *unfiring* (Glassblown) are not zero-sum the way
*Communion* and *Transcription* are. A walker can in principle be
*catalogued and unfired*: Curation files the unfiring as a non-
incrementing card; Glassblown reads the cataloguing as *fired-by-
paper, paste unaffected*.

Suggested clause for a future bible edit (near line 245):

> The zero-sum rule (Choir vs Palimpsest) is doctrinally specific
> to bodies whose materials are the same: living substrate (Choir)
> and the layer-record of living substrate (Palimpsest). Bodies
> with disjoint instruments — paper (Curation) and paste
> (Glassblown), tide (Brine) and clause (Concord) — admit walkers
> who reach high standing in both. Seam mechanics are what those
> walkers exercise.

**Cross-body symmetries, now five.** Adding to the four prior
(0001, 0101): **Curation ↔ Glassblown** — *un-bid* and *paste-held*
as structurally identical doctrines on opposite instruments.

R4's remaining seams: **Brine ↔ Concord** (remembered tense meets
clause-aspect; both bodies warp time) and **Thermoclaves ↔
Palimpsest** (the body of fire meets the body of layer; what does

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence:**

> *"Card-aspect is uncomposable with Glassblown hand-grammar — a
> sentence-final particle is itself a fired commitment."*

No archaism. No apostrophe-fantasy. No mythic-abstract verbs. A
grammatical-incompatibility statement: two bodies' marking systems
operate on different commitment-units, and the sentence-final
particle is definitionally the kind of unit Glassblown grammar
refuses. TES rewrite: *"Verily the speech of the Order of Cards
may not be made one with the speech of the Glassmakers, for the
syllable that closeth the sentence is itself a binding, and the
Order of Glass forsaketh all bindings save those which the molten
word hath made."* I did not write that. The seam vocabulary
(*un-bid card*, *transcribed-hand signature*, *aspect-stripped
speech*, *unfired shelf*, *card-aspect*, *figure*) names
instruments; the narrator reports without performing the bodies'
cadence — same restraint as Brine *remembered tense*, Concord
*clause-aspect*, Palimpsest *layer-grammar*, Glassblown
*hand-grammar*.
