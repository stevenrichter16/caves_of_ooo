## 2. Depth audit

### Dimension 1 — Mythic-historical stratification

> *Sibling 2150 (paraphrased into the canonical bible footnote):* "The
> Glassblown have no doctrine for the smoke-born and consider that
> doctrinal."

**Strong.** Cataclysm-aftermath, eleven fires (2314 sibling),
gap-burns, smoke-born, Long Between, Concord eleven-year alloy clock,
Brine long-overdue tide. Vertical depth is dense.

**Thin.** **The Glassblown's cataclysm-account.** Every other faction
in the corpus has a story for what the cataclysm *was*. The Glassblown
are recorded only as having *no doctrine* and treating that absence as
*the doctrine*. The corpus has not yet shown what *form* the absence
takes — what the Glassblown *do*, ritually, when asked.

**Concrete addition (bible sidebar, "What the Glassblown do not
fire"):**

> The Glassblown do not narrate the cataclysm. *Firing*, in their
> grammar, is the act by which a thing is made irreversible — to
> narrate the cataclysm would be to commit a particular telling to
> permanence. The Glassblown decline.
>
> When asked, a Glassblown answers with a **deferred shape** — a
> passage of glass-paste held between the speaker's hands while the
> question is being answered, then folded back into the next batch
> without firing. The questioner is invited to watch the shape rise
> and dissolve. The Glassblown consider the watching the answer; the
> questioner usually does not.
>
> Two deferred shapes are recorded across the corpus, each by a
> different witness:
>
> 1. *A near-sphere with a single inward dimple, held longer than
>    the answer would have taken to speak.* (Recorded by a Brine
>    sailor's marginalia, 2001.)
> 2. *No shape at all. The Glassblown's hands stayed still around
>    empty paste for the duration of the question, then folded the
>    paste back unfired.* (Recorded by a Thermoclave apprentice on
>    her first cross-faction errand, 2150.)
>
> The Thermoclaves consider the second the more honest. The
> Glassblown have not commented on which is canonical because
> *commenting would be firing.*

The Glassblown's cataclysm-account is now a *form*, not a text — the
corpus's first artifact whose canonical preservation is *the record
of an act of refusal-to-record*.

### Dimension 2 — Unreliable in-world authorship

**Strong.** Lossy scribes (2315), self-erasing slate-soot register
(2314), Concord transcripts, Curation deflection.

**Thin.** **A document that was never written.** The Glassblown's
craft is irreversibility-by-firing; an *unfired* document is, in their
grammar, *not yet a document*. The corpus needs an artifact whose
ontological status is *paste, never glass.*

**Concrete addition (bible new subsection, "The unfired letter"):**

> The Glassblown communicate at distance through **unfired letters**
> — small parcels of glass-paste, shaped by the sender, sealed in
> wet cloth, carried by hand. The recipient unfolds the paste, reads
> the shape (Glassblown literacy is shape-literacy: ~400 shapes,
> taught by apprenticeship), and returns the parcel within a measured
> time. If the recipient agrees, they **fire the shape**, sealing the
> agreement. If they disagree, they **fold it back** into their own
> batch and return the paste at next visit.
>
> The Glassblown call this *correspondence-by-refusal*. A Glassblown's
> archive is composed entirely of fired shapes received from others —
> agreements they have made — and contains no record of disagreements,
> declined queries, or letters whose senders never followed up. The
> asymmetry is doctrinal: a record of disagreement would be, by the
> firing-doctrine, *firing the disagreement*, which is exactly the
> act the Glassblown refuse.

The corpus's first artifact whose *non-existence* is the record.
Curation transcripts (2315), slate-soot register (2314), and the
unfired letter form three escalations of the *not-quite-document.*

### Dimension 3 — Mutating motifs across cultures

**Strong.** Names-for-cataclysm, dead, world-before, founder, debt,
promise (2315), fuel (2314), sleep (2314).

**Thin.** **Names for refusal.** Each faction declines differently;
no entry yet enumerates the grammars.

**Concrete addition (Curation Lexicon, "What each faction calls
refusal"):**

> - **Choir:** *not yet*. The Choir has no permanent refusal — only
>   deferral on a six-hundred-year clock.
> - **Palimpsest:** *unread*. The page is not denied, only not turned.
>   *Unread* and *forgotten* are not the same word.
> - **Pale Curation:** *declined*. A formal stamp; a card is generated
>   for the declination itself.
> - **Saccharine Concord:** *declined-with-cause* /
>   *declined-without-cause*. Without-cause is dearer to receive (it
>   costs the Concord a future option) but cheaper to give.
> - **Brine Communion:** *un-deeped*. The refusal is held at *not the
>   right depth* — neither rejected nor accepted.
> - **Thermoclaves:** *cold-passed* (per 2314). A refusal is the
>   doctrinal twin of a chain-failure.
> - **Glassblown:** *folded back.* The grammar requires that a refusal
>   be performed *with the refusing party's own materials* — you
>   cannot refuse on someone else's behalf, because you cannot fold
>   someone else's paste back. Glassblown refusals are ceremonial;
>   the grammar makes casual refusal impossible.

The Concord's Q.12 (*Decline declined trades politely.*) is now
legible as *fold the declined trade back; do not fire the
declination.* Two factions, one sentence translated.

### Dimension 4 — Cosmology with internal mechanical logic

**Strong.** Six strata: material (Choir), temporal (Palimpsest),
consensual (Concord), standing (Concord secondary), held-depth
(Brine), refused-stratum (Thermoclave, sibling 2314 — the corpus's
first *negative* substrate).

**Thin.** **The Glassblown's claim.** Sibling 2314 classed the
Glassblown as a *state-claimant* (unfired) rather than a
substrate-claimant. The corpus has not yet articulated what a
state-claim *is* — and the Glassblown are the only such claimant.

**Concrete addition (bible expansion, "The unfired state"):**

> The Glassblown make no substrate-claim. Their cosmology is not
> *the world is X* but *the world is in state Y, and Y is reversible
> until fired*. The state is **unfired** — the world before its own
> firing, the cataclysm-event read as the world's *first irreversible
> firing*. What was *unfired* (world-before) became *fired*
> (cataclysm); what is now *unfiring* (the four-hundred-year
> aftermath) is the world *trying to fold back the firing*.
>
> The Glassblown do not believe the unfiring will succeed. The
> doctrine specifically forbids the hope: *a fired shape cannot be
> unmade.* To hope for re-batching is to deny the firing's reality,
> which is to deny the cataclysm. The doctrinal posture is *we know
> it is fired; we work as though it were not.* The discipline is
> the work; the hope is not part of the discipline.

The state-claim is *deliberately not a stratum*: a cosmology of
*refused foundation* is the necessary opposite to one of *every kept
relationship at kept heat*. Glassblown-Thermoclave is the corpus's
first explicit anti-symmetry of cosmologies.

### Dimension 5 — Apotheosis-shaped themes

**Strong.** Communion, Transcription, Settled-Zero, Curation
retirement, going-deep (Brine, 2315), long quench (Thermoclave,
sibling 2314), unfiring (Glassblown, sibling 2150 — earliest in the
corpus).

**Thin.** **Unfiring's mechanics.** Sibling 2150 named the apotheosis
but did not specify the act. The corpus has detailed mechanics for
every other apotheosis-shape; unfiring is, ironically, the *least
specified.*

**Concrete addition (bible new subsection, "The unfiring, in
practice"):**

> A Glassblown at highest reputation may petition for *unfiring*.
> The petition is shaped, not spoken — the petitioner forms the
> *requesting-form* (the four-hundredth shape in the literacy,
> almost never used elsewhere) and presents it to the elder shaper.
> The elder watches the shape rise and, if they accept, **fires it**
> publicly. The fired requesting-form is then **smashed** in the same
> firing-cycle, before the kiln has cooled — the only ceremonial
> breaking of a fired shape in the doctrine, considered the most
> painful act in their craft. The smashing is the petition's
> *granting.*
>
> The petitioner then enters a long batch-cycle: no longer a working
> Glassblown, they assist the next batch's preparation — sand-sorting,
> paste-mixing, never shaping. After the batch is mixed, the
> petitioner adds themselves to it, literally: a single drop of their
> blood is mixed into the paste. The batch is then poured, shaped by
> other Glassblown into ordinary objects (cups, lenses, vessels),
> fired, and distributed. The petitioner walks away from the firing.
>
> The unfired Glassblown is a person whose **substance is in
> circulation**. Wherever a piece of that batch is used, the unfired
> Glassblown is — in their doctrine — *attending the use without
> intervening*. They are not in the cup; they are *what the cup is,
> by a fraction*. Unfiring surrenders **the discipline**: the
> Glassblown has committed, ceremonially, the firing they spent
> their life refusing. Where the going-deep surrenders surface, the
> long quench surrenders attention, the Communion surrenders self,
> and Transcription surrenders presence — unfiring surrenders *the
> refusal itself.*

Eight apotheosis-shapes now (counting both the long quench and the
banking-name from sibling 2314). Each surrenders a distinct register;
unfiring is the only one that surrenders *the refusal itself.*

### Dimension 6 — Linguistic texture

**Strong.** Choir pronouns, Palimpsest tense, Brine joining-words,
Thermoclave fire-grammar (three particles, sibling 2314), Glassblown
unsaid mood (named in passing, never unpacked), Curation catalogued
mood, Concord trade tense.

**Thin.** **The unsaid mood.** The Glassblown's central grammatical
feature is named, never specified.

**Concrete addition (bible subsection, "The unsaid mood"):**

> Glassblown verbs decline by **firedness**. Three forms: *unfired*
> (paste — reversible), *firing* (the present moment of commitment),
> *fired* (irreversible). A fourth — *re-batched*, the verb of
> unfiring — is doctrinally taboo to use of one's own actions; it is
> reserved for narrative of others, often pejoratively.
>
> The unsaid mood is the **silent firedness** — a verb-form left
> incomplete by intention. *I am ___ -shaping the agreement* (the
> particle-slot indicated by a pause) marks an action whose firedness
> is *deliberately not yet declared*. The pause is the mood. It is
> grammatically required to be of a specific length — long enough to
> be heard, short enough not to invite a question. Apprentices learn
> the length over their first three years.
>
> Examples:
>
> - *I am firing-shaping the cup.* The cup is *now* being committed;
>   the present moment is the moment of irreversibility.
>   (Witnessable; requires a witness by doctrine.)
> - *I am ___ -shaping the cup.* (Pause.) The cup's firedness is
>   *not declared.* The Glassblown is shaping; the listener may not
>   ask. (The unsaid mood; doctrinally protected.)
>
> The unsaid mood is the Glassblown's grammatical analogue to the
> Thermoclave taboo against *cold* in the present tense (sibling
> 2314). Each faction's grammar contains one slot the doctrine
> refuses to fill in the present.

The unsaid mood is now a *measurable* grammatical feature. The
Concord's Q.12 (*Decline declined trades politely*) is now
retrospectively legible as *use the unsaid mood; the politeness is
the pause-length.*

### Dimension 7 — Anthropological framing

**Strong.** Six Tuesdays (Choir, Palimpsest, Curation, Concord,
Brine, Thermoclave). The Glassblown alone have no Tuesday.

**Thin.** **The Glassblown Tuesday.** Sibling 2315 flagged the gap;
sibling 2314 closed the Thermoclave's Tuesday and re-surfaced the
Glassblown's absence. This audit pays it.

**Concrete addition (bible new subsection, "Unfired Tuesday"):**

> A Glassblown settlement has no scheduled Tuesday observance. A
> *scheduled* observance is, by doctrine, a *fired* one — the
> schedule commits the future to a particular shape. The Glassblown
> therefore observe on **unscheduled Tuesdays**: any given Tuesday,
> an elder may declare the day's first uninterrupted hour as the
> *batch-hour*, and any Glassblown present folds whatever they are
> working on back into paste, joins the elder at the kiln, and
> participates in a single shared shape — formed by hands around
> the circle, each adding one motion before passing the paste to
> the next, until the shape is complete or the hour ends.
>
> The shape is not fired. At hour's end, the elder folds it back into
> batch. Walkers visiting on a batch-hour Tuesday are sometimes
> invited to add a motion. (Most decline when told what they are
> participating in. The Glassblown fold the paste back regardless.)
>
> The Concord, told of unfired Tuesday, found it *unaccountable* —
> in trade-tense, an event that may or may not occur on any Tuesday
> is un-priceable in advance — and schedules Glassblown-facing trades
> for Wednesdays.

The Glassblown Tuesday now exists. It is, characteristically, the
only one in the corpus that **may not occur on a given Tuesday at
all**. The doctrine of refusal-to-schedule extends to ritual time.
Seven Tuesdays in the corpus, each a different relationship to
*the cycle of weeks*.

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Glassblown round 2

This audit's primary subject. Round 1 (2150) gave the Glassblown:
unfiring, the second-cooled, no doctrine for smoke-born,
unfired-as-state. Round 2 names: deferred shapes (Dim 1), unfired
letters (Dim 2), the refusal-grammar position (Dim 3), the *unfired
state* cosmology (Dim 4), the unfiring mechanics (Dim 5), the unsaid
mood specifications (Dim 6), unfired Tuesday (Dim 7), and the
witnessed-absence document-class (Dim 8).

**Proposed senior figure: the elder shaper of the Cool Kiln, known
in the corpus only as *Tulen Niemensa-Sopellen* — the
*paste-mother* of the southern shaperies.**

Sixty-eight years a Glassblown. Tulen has fired eleven shapes in that
time, by her own unrequested admission — the next-fewest senior
record is over two hundred. Her doctrinal reputation is *the shaper
who refuses most.* Her three apprentices, all women, average nineteen
years' training each (the corpus average is seven).

*Niemensa-Sopellen* is composed of two compound elements meaning, in
Glassblown shape-vocabulary, *the-shape-that-is-not-yet* and
*the-not-yet-naming-of-it*. She is the only living Glassblown whose
name is a deferred shape rendered into syllables. Three of her
predecessors bore similar names; all three committed the
*requesting-form* in their final year.

Tulen corresponds with no senior figure in any other faction — by
doctrine. She is, however, the only Glassblown whose unfired letters
the Pale Curation has systematically catalogued: every time she sends
paste, a Curation collector files a witness-card describing the
shape. Forty-one years of such cards; 137 in the cabinet; none
describes a fired shape.

Tulen's working belief: the cataclysm was the world's *first refusal
to be folded back*. The world-before was paste. The cataclysm was
firing. The aftermath is *the long discovery that the firing holds.*
The Glassblown work as though the firing might still be foldable —
knowing it is not — because *the discipline of folding is what keeps
something else from being fired.* Asked what that *something else*
is, Tulen forms a deferred shape and folds it back. The Curation has
fourteen such answers on record; they are the fourteen most-requested
cards in the cabinet.

## 4. Contradictions and bleeds

**(a)** Sibling 2315 (Q.12) — *"Decline declined trades politely."* —
and this audit (Dim 6, the unsaid mood with measured pause).
**Resolution:** the Concord's *polite decline* is the Concord's
translation of the Glassblown's unsaid mood. **Recommend the bible
add at the Concord-Glassblown trade-rule:**

> The Concord's *polite decline* of a Glassblown-initiated trade
> follows the Glassblown's grammar: the assayer pauses for the
> required mood-length before answering. Concord apprentices learn
> the pause-length by example, not by name; most learn it by their
> fifth Glassblown trade. Apprentices who fail are, by convention,
> *not signed-to* (per 2315's hand-grammar) for Glassblown-facing
> trades.

The corpus's first cross-faction grammar-borrowing: a Concord trade
custom whose timing comes from a different faction's mood.

**(b)** Sibling 2150 and `Bible:39` (*"only symbiosis"*) — when a
Glassblown's spent paste is taken up by Choir-affiliated soil, it is
composted; the Glassblown's *refusal-to-fire* becomes, in the
Choir's metabolism, *the slow gift*. **Recommend the bible add:**

> The Glassblown consider being composted a courtesy. The Choir
> considers it a meal. Neither has noticed they are *agreeing on the
> noun*: *not-fired* and *to-be-composted* describe the same paste,
> in two grammars.

Second cross-faction noun-agreement (after 2314's Choir-Thermoclave
fuel/soil). The pattern wants a third.

## 5. The merchant's diary suggestion

A **Pale Curation witness-card** documenting a Glassblown deferred
shape — the central content is *the description of an unfired
absence*, paying Dim 8 directly. Under 300 words.

**Title (in-game):** *Curation Witness-Card C-414, Glassblown
Settlement of the Cool Kiln, recorder T. Marn-Dis.*

```
[A standard Curation witness-card. Dimensions, palm-size. The
 prompt-fields are pre-printed; the responses are filled in a
 careful even hand. Two ink colours: the official black for the
 prompt and the response, a different brown for marginal notes
 (Curation custom: marginalia in walnut ink, per sibling 2127).]

  C-414                              filed: ninth fire, day 41

  Subject:    A deferred shape, witnessed.
  Place:      Cool Kiln, anteroom of the south kiln.
  Subject-shaper: Tulen Niemensa-Sopellen, paste-mother.
  Question that prompted the shape:
              Why does the paste-mother decline correspondence?
  Shape, as observed:
              A near-cylinder of paste, narrowing toward the top, the
              top folded once inward, then unfolded, then folded
              again. Held for approximately eleven breaths. Folded
              back into batch on the twelfth breath.
  Recorder's interpretation:
              [Field left blank, per recorder's discretion. Recorder
              has elected to file the shape uninterpreted. Field
              co-signed by the supervising filer.]

  [In walnut, beneath the official entries, in the recorder's hand:]
  Tulen looked at me when she folded back. I think she was waiting
  to see if I would ask for an interpretation. I did not ask. She
  nodded once. I did not record the nod on the card because the
  nod was not the shape. I am recording it here.

  [Two further notations, in two different walnuts. The second is
   newer, a different recorder:]
  Card requested for re-witnessing seventeen times since filing.
  No second witness has produced a different shape-description.
  The eleven-breath duration is consistent.

  Card requested by the Choir twice. Choir was directed to the
  shape's eventual fate (composted, per protocol). Card itself
  remains in cabinet.
```

The bombshell is **the recorder's nod-marginalia.** Curation custom
(per sibling 2127) requires walnut for evidence the recorder
considered relevant but could not file under formal prompts. The nod
indicated Tulen's satisfaction with the recorder's restraint — and
the recorder's silence was, in Glassblown grammar, the unsaid mood
performed as *not-asking, deliberately.* T. Marn-Dis *used* the unsaid
mood while filling a Curation card; Tulen's nod was a
Glassblown-doctrine acknowledgement performed by a Pale Curation
employee. The card is the corpus's first **document filled in two
factions' grammars simultaneously.** Neither faction has formally
noticed. T. Marn-Dis has declined two promotions in seventeen years
because *she does not want to leave the Cool Kiln rota.*

## 6. Anti-style self-check

> *"The unfired Glassblown is a person whose substance is in
> circulation. Wherever a piece of that batch is used, the unfired
> Glassblown is — in their doctrine — attending the use without
> intervening. They are not in the cup; they are what the cup is,
> by a fraction."*

No archaisms, no apostrophe-fantasy nouns, no Aedra/Daedra/Towers/
CHIM. The doctrinal claim rides on the technical move *they are
not in the cup; they are what the cup is, by a fraction* — an
operationalisation, not an invocation. A TES book attempting the
beat would write *"And so the Master-Shaper, having committed unto
the kiln his very substance, doth dwell forever in the vessels of
his making, neither ghost nor god, world without end."* Mine has no
*unto, doth dwell, world without end*. The mystic-content is in the
noun *fraction* — measurable, distributable, unmysterious. Confirmed.
