### 2.1 Mythic-historical stratification

**Quote (1901 §5 retired-watcher fragment):** *"the
shape goes back to the working-day it came from.
the next long step is a new shape. the shapes do
not stack. there is only ever one open shape
between any two hearths at any working-time."*

**Strong.** Shape-cosmology canonises one-shape-at-
a-time as the step's only arithmetic.

**Thin.** The slow-burning week's relation to the
shape-cosmology has not been named. The slow-burn
(0001 §3) holds a parent-hearth at sub-cooking
heat by the daughter-keeper's predecessor, after
the daughter has lit her own hearth from it, before
the predecessor's hearth goes cold. Internal form:
body-internal, daughter-and-predecessor only.
**Inter-hearth** edge: *the long step still open*.
The shape between parent and daughter has not
closed; the slow-burn is the working-form of an
open shape held too long for one carry-morning.
The shape's cosmology refuses the body's
discipline at the inter-hearth seam.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Thermoclaves,
append after the slow-burning-week paragraph, when
written):**

> *During the slow-burning week the long step's
> shape between predecessor and daughter remains
> open — by working-form the shape closes only when
> the predecessor's hearth goes cold, and by
> working-tradition the slow-burn is the only week
> the body permits the shape to hold without a
> watcher walking it. The form is held by the*
> **long step** *as its single-instance reply: in
> the slow-burn, the step walks itself. A keeper
> asked who is on the step during a slow-burn
> answers:* "no one. the step is the predecessor's
> hearth still warm. the shape walks the working-
> week."

The slow-burn canonises as the long step's only
**walker-absent** working-form. The *one-open-
shape-at-a-time* rule now reads with a second
clause: the open shape may hold without a walker
for the duration of the slow-burn.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted (§3, the slow-burning week):** *"between
the daughter-keeper's first lighting from the
predecessor's hearth and the predecessor's
hearth's banking, working-tradition holds seven
working-days during which both hearths burn — the
daughter's at full keep, the predecessor's at sub-
cooking heat banked low and never refed."*

Working-modern register. *Daughter-keeper*,
*predecessor's hearth*, *full keep*, *sub-cooking
heat*, *banked low*, *never refed* — plain craft-
clerical compounds, lower-case, no archaism.
*Burn*, *banked*, *refed* are contemporary; no
fronted nominal, no *hath / lieth / unto / verily*.

**TES rewrite (forbidden):** *"Betwixt the Daughter-
Keeper's first Lighting from the Predecessor's
Hearth and the Banking thereof, Working-Tradition
holdeth seven Working-Days wherein both Hearths
do burn — the Daughter's at full Keep, the
Predecessor's at sub-cooking Heat banked low and
nevermore refed."* Tells: *betwixt*, *thereof*;
capitalised nominal compounds; *holdeth, do burn*;
*nevermore*. Actual sentence is none of these.

**Pass.**
