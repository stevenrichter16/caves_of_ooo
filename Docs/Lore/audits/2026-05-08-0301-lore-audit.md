# Lore Audit — 2026-05-08 03:01 CDT

**Ninety-second audit. Sitting 54.** 0201 closed
the Saccharine Concord on a structural-closure
finding and asked this sitting to test the
**Glassblown Diaspora**'s bidirectional via
medium-carrier on the extensibility-axis: does the
**journey-crucible** admit outside-body extension
by departing the diaspora's working-form, or does
the working-form contain it? — with the auxiliary
note that the Glassblown is the only body whose
medium *is* the carrier.

**Finding: a third value — *trivial*.** The
question does not parse at the Glassblown. When the
working-form's substrate-direction is *bidirectional
through the medium* (2201 §2.4), and the medium is
itself the carrier, *outside-body* becomes a
category the working-form cannot register. The
journey-crucible is not the body's medium leaving;
it is the body's working-form **walking on its own
ground**. A journeying glass-master lighting a
foreign cluster's kiln-stone with the carried flame
performs the bidirectional substrate-direction at
the same body–Curation membrane she would perform
it at home — the body's working-form *is* the kiln-
flame, and the kiln-flame travels. There is no
membrane to cross. The 0201 candidate — that the
journey-crucible departs the diaspora's working-
form — is a false premise refused by the working-
form's own grammar.

**Through-line refines a twelfth time.** The
extensibility-axis resolves at three of five
bodies: Communion **admits**, Concord **forbids**,
Glassblown **trivial** (axis-collapse). The third
value is structurally distinct from the first two:
admits/forbids presuppose a working-membrane;
trivial is the body that has *no membrane on this
axis*. Curation and Thermoclaves unsorted at 0301.

## 2. Depth audit

### 2.1 Mythic-historical stratification

**Quote (2201 §2.1):** *"the kiln-stone is the
hearthstone of the cluster's kiln, replaced
cumulatively across generations and never wholly,
holding fire-time the body cannot doctrinally
subject."*

**Strong.** Kiln-stone as ancestral working-place.

**Thin.** Kiln-stone is fixed, cumulative, cluster-
bound. The diaspora's second ancestral working-form
is the *journey-crucible* — a forearm-length iron
kiln-tool a journeying glass-master carries between
clusters, fitted with a long-throat flue and a
single firing-cup. The fixed kiln-stone and the
carried journey-crucible together hold fire-time
across two registers: *lineage-fire* (cumulative,
in stone) and *carry-fire* (single working-life, in
iron). Not two media — the same fire, held twice.

**Concrete enhancement (bible §The Glassblown
Diaspora — append after the kiln-stone
absorption-paragraph, when written):**

> *The journey-crucible is a forearm-length iron
> kiln-tool a journeying glass-master carries
> between cluster-kilns across her working-life.
> It holds carry-fire — a small flame the
> journeyman kindles at her home-cluster's kiln-
> stone at first journey-day and feeds across her
> travels with charcoal struck at home-rank. The
> carry-fire is not the kiln-stone's fire and is
> not separate from it; the working-form admits it
> as the kiln-stone's fire-time held outside the
> kiln-stone, in iron, for the working-life.*

Lineage-fire in stone is the body at rest; carry-
fire in iron is the body in motion; neither is
*outside the body* because the body **is** the fire.

### 2.2 Unreliable in-world authorship

**Quote (2201 §2.2):** *"patina is the held-back's
residue-authorship — what the held-back has
inscribed on the surface across the cooling-watch
without holding the cadence."*

**Strong.** Residue-authorship as the body's only

**Thin.** The journey-crucible's authorship-status
is unwritten. When a journeyman lights a foreign
cluster's kiln-stone with the carry-fire — the

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Glassblown Diaspora (the journey-crucible; the lighting-touch; the foreign cluster's first-plate; trivial on extensibility)

> ### The journey-crucible's working-form
>
> A journeying glass-master at first journey-day
> kindles a small flame at her home-cluster's
> kiln-stone, transfers it to the journey-
> crucible's cup with a single charcoal struck
> from the home wood-rank, and seals the long-
> throat flue. The journeyman feeds the carry-
> fire at every cluster-visit's wood-rank with
> the visited cluster's charcoal; the fire's
> substance accumulates across the journey-life,
> as the kiln-stone's substance accumulates
> across the lineage.
>
> ### The lighting-touch
>
> At a foreign cluster's working-day-first hour the
> journeyman lifts a single charcoal from the
> journey-crucible's cup with her right hand and
> places it against the cluster's tinder-cradle.
> No salutation, no working-ceremony. The cluster's
> seam-master strikes the tinder; the kiln-stone
> takes the working-day's first heat from the
> carry-fire. The lighting-touch is a single
> working-second; the journeyman steps back to the
> third witness-stand for the cooling-watch in the
> held-back's working-form (2201).
>
> ### The foreign cluster's first-plate
>
> The cluster's first-plate of that working-day is
> poured by the seam-master, on a kiln-stone whose
> first heat came from a fire the journeyman
> brought. The plate-card carries a third entry —
> *carry-fire-lit, journey-crucible from \[home-
> cluster name\]* — beneath the kiln-mark and the
> seam-mark, signed by neither. The plate is filed
> on the cluster's rear shelf; a copy of the
> plate-card travels to Curation, which files
> under double-rear-shelf accession, citing both
> the cluster's rear shelf and the Curation prov.
> shelf.
>
> ### What the body does not do
>
> The body does not file the journey-crucible
> itself, the carry-fire's substance, the
> journeyman's route between clusters, or the
> lighting-touch. At the journeyman's retirement
> the carry-fire is *poured back into the home-
> cluster's kiln-stone* across a single working-
> day's first working-hour — the reverse working-
> form of the first journey-day, no plate poured.
> The kiln-stone holds, after the pouring-back,
> the carry-fire's accumulated substance from
> every cluster visited. Lineage-fire and carry-
> fire are *the same fire after retirement*; the
> body's two registers close at the kiln-stone,
> not at the journey-crucible.

Six body-bound canonised cadence-authorities:
pour-stance, cooling-watch, patina-naming,
recital-cadence (stone-bound); lighting-touch,
pouring-back (carry-bound). The cadence-set

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted (§3, the journey-crucible's working-form):**
*"a journeying glass-master at her first journey-
day kindles a small flame at her home-cluster's
kiln-stone, transfers it to the journey-crucible's
cup with a single charcoal struck from the home-
cluster's wood-rank, and seals the crucible's
long-throat flue."*

Working-modern register. *Journeying glass-master*,
*first journey-day*, *home-cluster*, *kiln-stone*,
*journey-crucible's cup*, *single charcoal*,
*wood-rank*, *long-throat flue*, *seals* — plain
craft-clerical compounds, lower-case, no archaism.
*Kindles, transfers, seals* are contemporary; no
fronted nominal, no *hath / lieth / unto / verily*.

**TES rewrite (forbidden):** *"At her First Journey-
Day a Journeying Glass-Master doth kindle a Small
Flame upon the Hearthstone of her Home-Cluster's
Kiln, beareth it unto the Cup of the Journey-
Crucible, and sealeth the Long-Throat Flue."*
Tells: capitalised nominal compounds; *doth
kindle, beareth, sealeth*; *upon / unto*; fronted
*At her First Journey-Day*. Actual sentence is
none of these.
