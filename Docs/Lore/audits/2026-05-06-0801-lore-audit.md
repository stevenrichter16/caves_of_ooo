## 3. Faction asymmetry — Saccharine Concord (parallel rotation)

**Caveat.** The 0601 and 0607 sittings each rotated Concord and
produced working-record artifacts (a quarter-ledger, a working-

crystalises chemistry of bitter-information delivery* and
*the unsweetened-year as twenty-five-year corrective ritual*,

be additive. Reconcile against 0601/0607 before any absorption.

The JSON ships **the Dissolution** at `RotChoir.json:481`
(*"The Concord calls it 'the Dissolution' and sweetens their
grief with trade"*) and **sweetness** at `RotChoir.json:338`.
The 0404 working draft proposed *the unsweetened year* as the
cataclysm-name; per 0501 §4, JSON canonical wins.

### Etymology

*Saccharine* in Concord usage is not the noun for *sugar* (that
noun is *grain*). It is the **register** — the speech used at
the *first telling* of a death, debt, or parting. To speak
saccharinely is to sweeten the difficulty of a thing as you
say it.

This is not euphemism but chemistry. Bitter information delivered
without the register *crystalises* in the listener — sets,
hardens, will not move. Delivered saccharinely it *dissolves* —
moves, mixes, becomes carryable. *The Dissolution* (cataclysm-
name) is the same word: the cataclysm was the largest piece of
bitter information ever delivered, and the Concord's project is
to keep dissolving it across generations so it never crystalises
into something a culture cannot carry. *Concord* names *the
agreement struck between two parties who acknowledge they will
outlive the agreement.*

### Working ritual: the saccharine telling

Hard news is delivered in the *first sweetened sentence* —
three clauses: *warm preamble*, *bitter pivot*, *carry*.
Preamble brief and concrete (*"the wind has been gentle this
month"*); pivot unhedged (*"your son did not return from the
salt-route"*); carry the next-step (*"the salt-house holds his
marker"*). Skipping any clause is **breaking register**; a
factor who breaks register loses *standing-as-saccharine* until
re-trained. Their trade-credit runs on it.

### Mourning practice: the unsweetened year

Every twenty-five calendar-years the Concord declares an
*unsweetened year*. The saccharine register is withdrawn from
all Concord speech. Deaths are spoken flat; contracts read flat.
The year re-establishes the bitterness of bitter things — a
corrective against long-cycle sweetening-into-meaningless.
Children born in it are said to *speak under-grain* their whole
lives, inflexibly direct in a culture that treats indirection as
moral hygiene. This resolves the 0404 *unsweetened year* draft:
**the Dissolution** is the canonical cataclysm-name; the
unsweetened year is its recurring corrective. Compatible.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §3 etymology):**

> *"Bitter information delivered saccharinely* dissolves *— moves,
> mixes, becomes carryable."*

*Bitter information* is the bare noun (no capitalisation, no
allegorical fronting). *Dissolves* is italicised as a technical
Concord term, not mythologised. *Moves, mixes, becomes carryable*
is three flat working-verbs of the kind a factor's training
manual would use.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Truth, when borne sweetly upon the
tongue, is as sugar in the kettle of the soul — it doth dissolve
into the broth of memory, and become a thing that may be carried
in the heart of the listener even unto the end of his days."*
Tells: *Truth/Soul/Memory* capitalised as allegorical entities;
*kettle of the soul* is appositional mythologisation; *doth* is
pseudo-archaic verb-padding; *unto the end of his days* is a
high-register coda. The actual sentence: six lower-case nouns
and three verbs, the chemistry doing the rhetorical work.

Pass.
