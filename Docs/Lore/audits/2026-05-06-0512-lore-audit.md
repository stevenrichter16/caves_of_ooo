## 2. Cold-eye pass on the 0501 audit

### 2.1 🔵 The 0501 §4 collision-table is correct but understates Brine and Thermoclave status

**File:** `2026-05-06-0501-lore-audit.md` §4 collision-table.

The 0501 §4 collision-table classifies seven faction-rows.
*Brine* and *Thermoclaves* are listed as **"Compatible"** — the
JSON does not name them, the bible footer adds them, no
contradiction. Right reading of the local table.

The understatement: the JSON's silence on Brine and Thermoclaves
is *itself* a faction-relations claim, not a faction-relations
gap. The Choir's `OtherVersions` node lists Concord, Curation,
Glassblown — and **leaves out** the two factions whose working
medium is most distant from the Choir's reach (Brine on water
the Choir cannot cross per L116; Thermoclaves at heat the Choir
will not approach per audit-chain working position). The
omission encodes the Choir's geography of knowledge. Item 29-
revised, when shipped, doesn't just *fill* the omission — it
*re-codes* it as Choir-ignorance, which the 0501 §3 Brine
bridge already noted in passing (*"the JSON omission becomes
Choir-side ignorance, not authorial gap"*) but which the §4
table classified at the lower stake of *compatible.*

**Severity 🔵.** The 0501 closer's branch table for sitting 20
should foreground the *Choir-ignorance-as-fictional-payoff*
reading. Item 29-revised's absorption is not just hygienic; it
turns a JSON gap into characterisation.

### 2.2 🔵 The 0501 §5 marginalia includes one verifiable claim worth checking

**File:** `2026-05-06-0501-lore-audit.md` §5 sailor's marginalia.

The 0501 §5 marginalia asserts (parenthetically) that
*"a wave would be sung at the bow. The slow tide we sing
under, where the slow tide reaches."* This is a working-rule
the 0501 invents — fast event sung at bow, slow event under
keel. The rule is internally consistent and Anti-Style passes
on it. **But:** the bible's L19 cataclysm-frame (*"It did not
merely destroy... it overwrote reality itself"*) reads the
cataclysm as a **slow** event in cosmic register. The 0501's
*the long, slow tide, not a wave* keel-singing-under-the-tide
working-rule maps the bible's L19 directly onto the Brine's
working-grammar. **No collision** — but worth noting as a
*positive cross-reference*: the 0501 §5 is the first audit
artifact that reads the bible's L19 cataclysm-tempo (slow,
overwriting, not catastrophic) into a working faction-mechanic.

**Severity 🔵.** Useful as a graduation-pass anchor when the
0501 §5 marginalia (currently a candidate found-document, not
a bible-side item) is considered for absorption. The
keel-singing rule is the strongest existing precedent in the
chain for *bible cataclysm-tempo* mapping into *faction working
practice.* Item 30 (moderate-reputation paragraph) could
similarly cite L19's overwrite-tempo to ground the *fluency
loss* mechanic in cosmic register, not just gameplay register.

### 3.2 Item 27 — quintet contingency

The 0301 §2.7 quartet and the 0407 §3 quintet revision name five
working mediums as a coordinated set — implicitly claiming each
faction's medium has the same doctrinal weight. The JSON corro-
borates **only the Choir's spore-witness.** The other four
(Curation thinned-ink, Brine water-mass, Thermoclave heat-color,
Glassblown shards-via-edge-and-color) are wholly audit-
extrapolated.

This is not a contradiction. The JSON is Choir-side reportage;
the JSON does not narrate other factions' working-mediums
because the Choir does not have access to them. The medium-as-
reader claim about other factions can stand without JSON
corroboration the same way the bible's existing claims about
the Curation's archives (L95) and the Saccharine Concord's
sweetening-of-grief (L481, shipped) stand without working-
mechanic explication.

But the **strict reading** of hygiene item 2 — *grep the shipped
JSON before drafting any... cosmology artifact* — flags item 27
for one of two graduation-pass treatments:

  - **Treatment A (recommended):** explicit narrator-frame in
    the absorption text. The bible should *name* that the four
    non-Choir mediums are inferential; the Choir is the only
    medium-faction whose shipped reportage corroborates the
    medium-as-reader claim from the inside. *"Each faction's
    working medium is here named as the audit chain has read
    them; only the Choir speaks of its own medium in the shipped
    record"* — or something more bible-voiced. Cost: two extra
    sentences in the item 27 absorption text.
  - **Treatment B:** quintet ships without narrator-frame; the
    in-fiction reader takes each medium on its working-faith
    the way the bible takes the Concord's *Sweetening* on faith
    of one shipped JSON line.

**Recommendation:** Treatment A. The audit chain has spent
multiple sittings developing the four extrapolated mediums; an
honest narrator-frame protects the bible from a future-editor
asking *where does the Curation's thinned-ink come from?* The
honest answer is *the audit chain inferred it from the
Curation's working-pedagogy,* and the bible should not present
inferences as if they were corroborated.

### 3.3 Soft-tension: JSON L338 vs. audit-chain Glassblown doctrine

Separate finding, surfaced by the verification grep. JSON
`:338`: *"The Saccharine Concord offers sweetness. The Pale
Curation offers records. The Glassblown Remnant offers nothing
at all."*

The audit chain has developed the Glassblown's working-doctrine
substantively across multiple sittings: the cooling-watch and
twice-witnessed (0404 §3), the shard-cabinet and never-re-melt
rule (0407 §3), the *Last Heat* / *cold-glass years* idiom
(2101 §3). The Glassblown carry the **strongest doctrinal
prohibition the audit has named in any faction.** A faction with
that level of working-pedagogy is not, by any working reading,
*offering nothing at all.*

**Resolution.** The JSON L338 is **Choir-side reportage** — the
Choir's working assessment of what each faction *offers the
walker* (i.e., is willing to trade or share). The Glassblown's
*offers nothing at all* is the Choir reading the Glassblown's
public-side refusal-grammar (per 0407 §3 cross-reference) as
total. The Choir, by working position, does not see the
shard-cabinet, the cooling-watch, the never-re-melt rule —
those are inside the closed bench, behind the working-master's
stool. *The Glassblown offers nothing to outsiders* would be
literally accurate; the Choir's flat *offers nothing at all*
is shipped imprecision.

**Severity 🔵.** Not a contradiction — Choir-side reportage of a
secretive faction may legitimately under-describe. But worth
noting in any future Glassblown bible section: the JSON's L338
flat reading does not survive contact with the audit chain's
developed working-doctrine, and the bible's eventual Glassblown
sidebar should hold *the working faction is much fuller than
Choir reportage suggests* as standing positional context.

## 5. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §3.2):**

> *"The Choir is the only medium-faction whose shipped reportage
> corroborates the medium-as-reader claim from the inside."*

Working register, gloss-form. *Medium-faction* and *shipped
reportage* are the audit-chain's working hyphenates the way the
0404 used *bay-arrival* — flat procedural compounds. *From the
inside* is a working positional adverb the bible already uses
(*"how that belief shapes a Tuesday morning"* at the §6 prompt
register). The sentence's claim is procedural — about which
piece of evidence ships — not theological about which faction is
truer. Lower-case throughout. No allegorical capitalisation.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Of all the Mediums by which the
Factions read their fragmented World, only the Choir's own
voice — preserved in the very Spores from which it grew — speaks
the Witness of its own Reading: the others have no such
testament."* Tells: *Mediums*, *Factions*, *World*, *Spores*,
*Witness*, *Reading* (allegorical capitalisations); *fragmented
World* (mythic adjective-noun); *the very Spores from which it
grew* (relative-clause mythologisation); *no such testament*
(KJV-style negative coda). The actual sentence: *Choir*,
*medium-faction*, *shipped reportage*, *corroborates*, *the
inside* — five working-register terms, no allegorical noun-
phrase, the procedural rule (which evidence ships) doing the
work.

Pass.
