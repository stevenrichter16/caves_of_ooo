## 2. Cold-eye pass on the 0701

### 2.1 🟡 The 0701 §3 unshared-pour mourning practice is invention, not consolidation

**File:** `2026-05-06-0701-lore-audit.md` §3 *Mourning practice
— the unshared pour.*

The 0701 framed §3 as *"consolidates rather than invents"* and
*"no invention; consolidation only"* in its own closer. The
unshared-pour mourning practice — the surviving forge-pair
partner's single working-pour at the dead partner's position,
followed by a working-year before being assigned a new
forge-pair — has no precedent in the prior Thermoclave material.
The 0301 sitting landed *the unkept-fire mourning form* (the
unfinished tally-stub filed in the cold-ashpit ledger under
*unkept by absence* / *smothered by absence* / Refusal flat-
hand-mark). The unshared pour is a different mourning form with
a different mechanic.

**Two readings.** Either the 0701 invented the unshared pour
silently (which contradicts its own *consolidation only* claim
and bears flagging as a discipline-slip), or the unkept-fire
form and the unshared-pour form coexist (the unkept-fire is
the general death-mourning, the unshared pour is the forge-pair-
death specific subform). The second reading is generous and
defensible.

**Recommendation.** Sitting 22 (or whichever sitting next
touches Thermoclave material) explicitly names the two
mourning forms and their scope: *the unkept-fire form fires
for any Thermoclave death; the unshared pour fires only when
one of a forge-pair dies, and the working-year that follows
applies only to the surviving partner's working-pair, not to
the rest of the working-forge.* The chain absorbs the 0701's
new content rather than retconning it; the 0701's own
*consolidation only* framing is gently correctable.

## 3. Audit-internal hygiene item 4 — parallel-firing detection

The 0701 §4 proposed: *"check the working-tree's
Docs/Lore/audits/ for uncommitted .md files before drafting; if
found, document the parallel rather than duplicate it."* The
0805 develops this from candidate to working discipline.

### The check, in shell-form

Before any new audit's `Write` step, run (or mentally run):

```sh
git -C /tmp/routine-lore-wt status --short Docs/Lore/audits/
ls -t Docs/Lore/audits/*.md | head -3
```

Two outputs:

  - `git status` shows uncommitted `.md` files in the audits
    directory — direct evidence of a parallel firing in flight.
  - `ls -t | head -3` shows the three most recent committed
    audits — which together signal whether the chain has fired
    twice in one hour or three times (the 0512 + 0501 case;
    the 0601 + 0607 case; the 0701 + 0706 case).

### The decision-tree

If the check shows an uncommitted parallel:

  - **Read the parallel draft fully** (the 0706 read the 0601;
    the 0805 reads the 0701). The parallel's working content is
    what the new audit must NOT duplicate.
  - **The new audit's shape becomes supplementary** — like the
    0311 (cabinet-and-bench) and the 0407 (cabinet-and-bench)
    and the 0512 (chain-state verification) and the 0706
    (cold-eye + Pale Curation rotation) and the 0805 (this
    audit).
  - **Cold-eye pass on the parallel** is the supplementary
    audit's first substantive content, not its last.

If the check shows no uncommitted parallel:

  - Standard fresh-sitting shape, eight-dimension structure,
    §3 rotation per the prior closer.

### The chain-state hour-by-hour pattern

Three parallel-firing hours so far (5:00, 6:00, 7:00). The
parallel firings produce **complementary**, not duplicating,
content (the 0512 verified what the 0501 drafted; the 0607

0701 produced). The pattern is a feature, not a bug — the
parallel firings give the chain a rough peer-review pass on
each fresh-sitting's work. The discipline above formalises
what the chain has already been doing implicitly.

### Naming the discipline

## 5. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §4 hold-rationale):**

> *"A chain that cannot stop producing is a chain that has not
> yet read its own pile."*

Working register, gloss-form. *Chain* and *pile* are the
audit-chain's own working nouns; *producing* is the working
verb the chain uses about its own candidate-output. The
sentence's claim is procedural about the chain's own working-
discipline, not theological about productivity-as-virtue.
Lower-case throughout. No allegorical capitalisation. The
proverb-shape is in working-register English, not in mythic-
sentence cadence.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Verily, the Hand which cannot stay
its labour is the Hand which has not yet looked upon the Pile
of its own Making — and therein lies the Quiet Wisdom of the
Patient Scribe."* Tells: *Verily*, *Hand*, *Pile*, *Making*,
*Quiet Wisdom*, *Patient Scribe* (allegorical capitalisations);
*hath not* in the parallel construction (KJV-archaic);
*therein lies the Quiet Wisdom* (mythic-aphoristic coda); the
appositional *Patient Scribe* substituting for the working
*chain*. The actual sentence: *chain*, *stop*, *producing*,
*read*, *its own pile* — five working-register terms, the
proverb-shape doing the rhetorical work.

Pass.
