## 3. Faction asymmetry — Glassblown silence-discipline (narrow rotation)

The 0404 produced a substantial Glassblown rotation: *Remnant*
double-etymology, cooling-watch ritual, twice-witnessed mourning
practice. Pre-write verification confirmed those frames cover
the working-craft side comprehensively. The one Glassblown angle
the 0404 did not surface — and the bible/JSON imply but never
state — is the **silence-discipline.**

**Source data.** The JSON ships two Glassblown lines:

  - L338: *"The Glassblown Remnant offers nothing at all."*
  - L481: *"The Glassblown call it 'the Burning' and refuse to
    speak further."*

The bible adds nothing. *Refuse to speak further* is a working-
practice. The 0404 did not address how it operates.

### The unspoken-after — narrow rotation

The Glassblown silence about *the Burning* is not a taboo. It is
**a working-form** with the same shape as the cooling-watch and
the twice-witnessed: it is procedurally observed, it has rules,
and breaking it has a recorded consequence.

**The rule.** A Glassblown practitioner names *the Burning* once
in their working-life — in the **first-firing**, the day they
are first permitted to draw molten material from the working
furnace. The naming is witnessed by the practitioner who relieved
their cooling-watch the day before. It is the only context in
which the word is spoken inside the working. Outside the
first-firing, the working-form is **unspoken-after** — the word
is not used, by anyone, ever again.

**Why.** The 0404 cooling-watch established *reading the strip is
the working.* The silence-discipline extends the same logic in
the opposite direction: **not-reading the strip is also the
working** — when no strip is available, the only honest practice
is silence. *The Burning* left no working-strip; no cooling-
pattern, no record, no relief-rotation. To speak of it without a
strip is **to fabricate strip-data** — a violation worse than
failing to file. Silence is the procedurally-honest position in
the absence of the artifact that would make speech procedurally-
possible.

**Breaking.** A Glassblown who speaks *the Burning* outside the
first-firing is **un-named** — working-name withdrawn, no draw
from the working furnace until re-trained, and their cooling-
watch entries from the prior working-month are **re-witnessed**
by another practitioner (in case the un-named practitioner's
reading was also fabricated). The Glassblown have no word for
*forgiveness* or *re-counting* (cf. Thermoclave 0701 §3); they
have **re-witnessing** — *the strip is re-read by other eyes;
the prior reading stands or falls on its own evidence.*

Compatible with 0404 working-rules; extends silence-handling
without inventing new mechanisms.

## 4. Contradictions and bleeds — additive evidence for the 0601 L39 finding

**Acknowledgment.** The 0601 audit (§4) found that bible L39

This sitting does not duplicate that finding. It supplies
**additive evidence and a competing fix** that the 0601 did not
have access to.

### Additive evidence — the Choir's primary voice contradicts L39 too

The 0601 used Mogu (a sub-faction NPC voice) as contradicting
evidence. **The contradiction also lives in the Choir's primary
narrator-dialogue**, not just in the named-NPC sub-voices.

**JSON L88 (`OldestTruth` node, primary `ChoirTendril_1`
voice):**

> *"That nothing is lost. Everything that dies feeds what comes
> next. The old world did not end -- it was composted. **We
> remember everything we have ever eaten.**"*

**JSON L565 (Choir comparing self to Palimpsest):**

> *"They preserve by remembering. We preserve by consuming.
> **The result is the same -- nothing is truly lost.**"*

This is significant because the 0601's recommended fix —
reframing L39 as the *Choir's self-account* — is **incompatible
with the Choir explicitly disowning it.** The Choir does not
say *we lose what we eat*; it says *we remember everything we
have ever eaten.* If L39 becomes the Choir's self-account, the
JSON becomes the Choir contradicting itself across files. The
0601's fix resolves bible-vs-NPC bleed but creates a stronger
bible-vs-primary-voice bleed.

### Competing fix — path 3 (mark L39 as Palimpsest-reading)

A cheaper resolution: mark L39 as **Palimpsest-reading**, not as
*Choir self-account*. The five-word edit:

> *"A rusted sword composted by the Choir, **as the Palimpsest
> reads it**, loses its history — who forged it, who wielded it,
> where it fell..."*

**Why this fixes more than the 0601 path.** *Loses its history*
is structurally a **Palimpsest claim**, not a Choir claim. The
Choir denies loss; the Palimpsest asserts it. The 0601's path
attributes a Palimpsest-shaped sentence to the Choir's mouth —
which does not match the JSON. This sitting's path attributes it
to its actual speaker and preserves both bible and JSON positions
intact. Five words at L39 vs. a paragraph at L116; cheaper, more
accurate, doesn't add bible material (honoring the 0801 hold).

**Recommended sequencing.** Item 40 first (cheapest). Then 0801
§2.4 (its authority depends on this resolution). The 0601 §2.7
paragraph at L116 retains independent value as
*anthropological framing of how persons survive integration*
and may absorb separately later.

**Open as item 40.** Resolution finding, not pile-adding.

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §3 silence-discipline):**

> *"Silence is the procedurally-honest position in the absence
> of the artifact that would make speech procedurally-possible."*

*Procedurally* is a working-grammar adverb (not capitalised, not
mythologised). *The artifact* is a bare definite article (no
*Sacred Artifact*, no allegorical fronting). The sentence states
an epistemic working-rule the way a craft manual would: silence
is the right move when the records aren't there. Three flat
clauses, the rule doing its own rhetorical work.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Silence, beloved, is the holy hush
that descendeth upon the faithful when the Sacred Strip lies
absent from its proper bench."* Tells: *beloved* address;
*holy hush*, *Sacred Strip* allegorically capitalised;
pseudo-archaic *descendeth*. The actual sentence: epistemological
language, no proper nouns, the reasoning doing the work.

Pass.
