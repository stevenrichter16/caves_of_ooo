## 3. Faction asymmetry — Brine Communion (rotation)

The Brine Communion is named in the bible at the
single line L208 *("they don't hate each other
the way the Thermoclaves and Brine Communion do")*
and referenced obliquely in the routine-prompt's
*"a Brine Communion sailor's marginalia in a
salvaged book."* It has not been built out beyond
those pointers. This sitting lands the working-form
for one register and an etymology for one of its
standing rituals.

### Etymology — the Salt-Line

The Brine Communion's central practice is the
**Salt-Line** — a line of dry salt drawn around
the crock at the sealing-day, the verb-form *to
draw the salt-line* on the sealing-day, *to hold
the salt-line* during transit, *to lay the salt-
line down* at the bench-end. The line is not
ornamental; the Brine reads it as the working-
form's *boundary-discipline* — the crock is
read as *standing-stable* only while the salt-
line is held intact. A crock whose salt-line is
broken in transit is filed as *dropped*, the
sailor's working-week is filed as *line-down*,
and the next shipment carries a doubled salt-line
to make the dropping-form legible at the bench-
end.

The Brine etymology for *line* derives from a
pre-cataclysm coast-vocabulary in which the noun
meant *the inter-tidal mark*, the line on a sea-
wall where the salt-water reaches at the highest
tide. The Brine retained the noun across the
cataclysm without retaining the sea-wall. The
working-form's only living trace of the inter-
tidal mark is the salt-line drawn on the crock —
the salt that *would have been* deposited by the
tide is now drawn by the sailor. A Brine
apprentice asked what *line* originally meant
answers: *the mark the sea draws on the wall*.
The apprentice does not know the wall is gone.
The Brine holds the noun across a substrate-shift
the practice has not named.

### The salt-line manifest

A Brine sailor's working-form is the **salt-line
manifest** — a folded shipment-card carried crock-
side, on which the sailor records four readings
per shipment-day: *sealing-day* (the crock's
working-week of preparation), *salt-quotient*
(the salt-line's mass at sealing, recorded in
working-grains), *swell-watch* (the transit
working-day's sea-state, recorded in three named
swells: *flat / standing / breaking*), and
*bench-end* (the salt-line's state at the
shipping-bench's far-end-receipt). The columns
are not events; they are *positions in the line's
holding*. A manifest filed at week-end with
*flat* swell-watch and *unbroken* bench-end is
filed as *line-held*; a *breaking* swell-watch
with *unbroken* bench-end is filed as *line-held-
through*; a *flat* swell-watch with *broken*
bench-end is filed as *line-dropped-without-cause*
— the working-form's most-flagged result, because
the dropping cannot be traced to substrate.

### The crock's silence

The Brine sailor does not speak during the crock's
sealing-minute. A single bell at the salt-line's
drawing, a single bell at the crock's sealing,
silence between. The sailor who speaks during the
sealing-minute is filed as *line-undrawn*
regardless of the salt-quotient. The working-
form's first lesson: **the salt-line is read by
the crock, not heard by the sailor**. The Brine's
sealing-discipline and the Concord's standing-
discipline and the Glassblown's cooling-watch
are working-form parallels at three media — salt,
sweetness, and glass — all reading slow-change
at three-or-four positions, all filing on paper
at week-end, all silent during the central
minute. (The pattern-triple sits *beside* the
slate; the only *medium*-sharing pair among them
is Concord-Brine, via the cane-syrup shipment.)

## 4. Contradictions and bleeds

**Bleed 1 — bible L208 *"they don't hate each
other"* vs. seam-admission criterion.** The bible
collapses *hatred* and *arbitration*; the slate's
edge-criterion separates them. *Hatred* is the
working-face of arbitration at cosmic-tier; *non-
hatred* is the working-face of non-arbitration.
The Thermoclave-Brine war is arbitration; the
Choir-Palimpsest non-comprehension is non-
arbitration. Suggested L208 supplement (per §2.1
above): the binary *hate/not-hate* should refine
to *arbitrate/not-arbitrate*, with hatred named
as the cosmic-tier working-face of arbitration.

**Bleed 2 — bible L24 *"some try to ignore"
[the cataclysm]* vs. Brine *line*-etymology.**
The bible's faction-axis enumerates relations to
the cataclysm: preservation, consumption, ignorance,
production. *Ignorance* is named as a working-
form. The Brine's working-form for the verb *line*
is **the working-form-internal forgetting of the
substrate-shift** — the apprentice answers *the
mark the sea draws on the wall* without knowing
the wall is gone. This is not ignorance of the
cataclysm; it is *ignorance held inside a working-
discipline that retains the verb*. The Brine
working-form has a clause for the verb but no
clause for naming the substrate-shift the verb has
crossed. Suggested L24 supplement: *"(A fifth
relation is held by the working-forms of mid-
tier factions: substrate-shift retained inside
the working-vocabulary, the substrate's vanished
working-bodies un-named in the discipline. The
Saccharine Concord's *stand* and the Brine
Communion's *line* are two cases.)"*
