### 2.2 🟡 The 0601 §2.4 Palimpsest enforcement rule has a small mechanical edge-case

**File:** 0601 §2.4 (Palimpsest enforcement rule, post-L125).

references will not take at all."* The edge case: what counts

## 3. Faction asymmetry — Pale Curation rotation

Last full Curation rotation: 0201 (negative-catalogue, negative-
blank, two-hand rule in negative). Earlier rotations: 1701
(standing-junior drawer), 2301 (firm-ink / field-ink, Hall-
Seventh duplicate memo). The 0706 develops a register the prior
rotations have gestured at without working: the **request-rider**
— the Curation working-position responsible for inter-faction
delivery of release-slips and request-forms.

### The request-rider's working-bag

A standing leather satchel, undyed (a Curation rule: undyed
leather receives no fading-test, so the bag's age is read by
wear-pattern, not colour). Inside: the rider's standing
form-book (every request-form in stub form, perforated); a
pouch of writing-charcoals in two grades (dark for receiving
signatures; pale for the rider's own marginalia); a sealed
bottle of witness-water dated at departure; a leather thong
for tying received artifacts to the bag's inside loops; a
folded grease-cloth for protecting received artifacts from
inner seams. No working-knife — a request-rider is unarmed
by working-position; the rider's safety is the form's
authority, not the rider's hand.

### The request-rider's working-day

A single delivery loop, never a round-trip. Departure one bell
after dawn, foot or light-cart, one or two deliveries (juniors
one), return by the next standing-time the senior nominates.

The working pace is *deliberate slow* — the rider walks at the
speed a senior cataloguer reads at a working-table. Walking
faster is read as *appearing harried,* which receiving factions
read as *the Curation has lost its standing patience.* The
Curation does not lose its standing patience by working-
position. A rider who reaches the receiving counter sweating
is, by working-rule, **refused** — returns to the home depot
with request undelivered, files a note explaining the haste,
tries again the next standing-day.

### The receiving counter's working-form

Each faction has agreed (or not) to a standing counter-position
the rider approaches. **Concord:** *receiving-bench* (one factor
on duty per market quarter). **Glassblown:** *bench-side wicket*
(the working-master hears; the apprentice does not).
**Brine:** *shoaling-house door* (the rider does not enter).
**Thermoclaves:** *forge-mouth* (the working-forger hears
between strikes — nods or shakes, no spoken reply).
**Choir:** no agreed counter — the request is left in a small
*spore-warding* basket at the cave-mouth; response, if any,
appears next morning in the same basket as a single mycelial
token.

**Palimpsest:** a counter the audit cannot describe. Riders
refer to it as *the standing place* — the location is not
always the same; the rider knows when the counter is present
by the absence of any other description fitting the location
they have arrived at. The Palimpsest's working response, when
it comes, is a marginalium that appears in the rider's own
form-book, in a hand the rider does not recognise but written
in the rider's own working-charcoals.

### Receipt-slip and no-show

A successful delivery generates a *receipt-slip* (the
receiving faction's countersignature). A *no-show* (no
counter-position, no countersignature) generates a *no-show
slip*, filled by the rider from their own marginalia, filed
in a separate ledger at the home depot.

The Curation's no-show ledger is the most-read working-document
in the depot — its standing record of which factions are *not,
this season, available for inter-faction working trade.*

## 6. Anti-style self-check

**Quoted sentence (from §3, request-rider working-day):**

> *"A rider who reaches the receiving counter sweating is, by
> working-rule, refused."*

Working register, gloss-form. *Working-rule* is the flat
procedural compound the Curation uses about its standing
working-positions. *Refused* is a working clerical verb — the
rider's request is not received, the rider returns. The
sentence's claim is procedural about a working-position; the
sweating is read by the receiving faction as *the Curation has
lost its standing patience*, and the working-rule prevents the
appearance. Lower-case throughout. No allegorical capitalisation.

TES rewrite (forbidden): *"Whomsoever among the Pale Riders
should arrive at the Receiving Hall in the very Sweat of his
Travel, he shall be Turned Away — for haste betokens Want, and
the Pale Order knows no Want."* Tells: *Whomsoever*; *Pale
Riders*, *Receiving Hall*, *Sweat*, *Travel*, *Pale Order*,
*Want* (allegorical capitalisations); *betokens* (KJV-archaic);
*Turned Away* (mythic-passive). The actual sentence: *rider*,
*receiving counter*, *working-rule*, *refused* — four working-
register terms, the rule (sweating → refusal) doing the work.
